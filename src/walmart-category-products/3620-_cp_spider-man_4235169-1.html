About Spider-Man - Walmart.com

Quick guide to entering the Spider-Verse

Marvel's Spider-Man: Far From Home has fans of all ages tapping into their spidey senses. Picking up Peter Parker and his alter ego's story where Avengers: Endgame leaves off, this new movie packs in all the action and adventure you crave. And with our lineup of toys, clothing, and home goods, you can bring some of that fun home with you. Want to know more about how you can be a part of the Spider-Verse? Check out this quick guide as you start shopping.

Spider-Man toys and costumes

Get into all the Spidey fun with our huge assortment of action figures, nerf blasters and costumes. With plenty of figures and vehicles to get the action started, and all the accessories you need to round out a collection — like web slingers and playsets — there's a whole Spider-Verse of fun to take home! Costumes and masks make for even more role-playing fun and let little minds run wild imagining themselves as Peter Parker getting ready for adventure!

Spider-Man for home

Wave your Spidey-flag loud and proud when you bring some Spider-Man home with you. There are many ways to include Spider-Man in your home's decor. From sheet sets and comforters to toy organizers and tents, we have lots of home goods just for Spider-Man fans like you. No matter if you're looking for bean bags, comforters, curtains, or night-lights, consider these features to pick the perfect option:

  • Colors: Are you looking for something with a light background and a subtle Spidey-pattern? Or do you prefer bright, bold hues and large graphics?
  • Size: Particularly important for curtains, sheets, and comforters, opting for home goods in the right size is essential. Note your bed size, measure windows, and measure floor space to make sure whatever you choose will fit exactly like it's supposed to.
  • Age: If you're buying for yourself, that's easy-peasy. But if you're getting a gift, make sure you choose age-appropriate items.

Spider-Man clothing

Jammies, tees, and costumes are just the tip of the iceberg when you're browsing through Spider-Man clothing options. How can you choose? Here are a few tips:

  • Size: Note whether the item is for babies, toddlers, big kids, or adults to make sure you're picking items that are sized correctly.
  • Material: Cotton and cotton blends offer superior breathability, making them great options for nightwear, undies, and tees. Polyester typically provides excellent water-resistance, making it a great option for swimwear and workout gear.
  • Colors: It's all about personal style. Choose the colors that make your Spidey-senses tingle.
