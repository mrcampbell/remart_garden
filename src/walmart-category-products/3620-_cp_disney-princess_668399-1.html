About Disney Princess - Walmart.com

Everything You Need for Your Little Disney Princess Fan

It's hard to find a little girl who doesn't love Disney Princesses, and many boys are big fans too. At Walmart, we have what you need to please your little Disney Princess fan. Shop by category, or shop for gear based on your child's favorite character, whether it's Ariel, Aurora, Belle, Cinderella, Moana, Pocahontas, Tiana, Snow White, Rapunzel, Pocahontas, Jasmine, Merida, or Mulan. Our Disney Princess shop is full of so many fun and fresh items at everyday low prices that you're sure to find something even for the princess lover who has everything.

Toys and bikes: You can't go wrong with choosing among the Princess toys, bikes and ride-on toys for your child. Pink cars, bikes with training wheels, and carriages are perfect for active kids who love to play outside. Your little one can show off some princess pride while learning to pedal for the first time up and down the sidewalk or taking the carriage for a spin up the driveway. The bikes and ride-on toys are available for toddlers and preschoolers.

If you're looking for something a little smaller, you can shop for Disney Princess toys by brand, character, price, and other categories. Choose a doll version of your child's favorite Princess, whether it's a baby doll for younger kids or a fashion doll for older ones. Many dolls come with accessories, and some have additional features, like a swimming Ariel or a singing Moana. Older kids will enjoy sets that allow them to build their favorite castles and scenes from their favorite Disney movies as well as Disney-themed versions of their favorite board games. And kids of all ages may enjoy dollhouses, make-believe sets, or collectibles. There are Disney Princess toddler toys for the littlest fans too.

Clothing and costumes: If your little one already has more toys than he or she can play with, take a look at our Disney Princess-themed clothing and costumes. From pajamas to bathing suits and dresses to underwear, your little one can keep a favorite Princess close all day long. And if mom or big sister is a fan too, you'll find pajama sets and other fun clothing items for adults featuring everyone from Ariel to Cinderella. Parents who love Disney Princesses can also start their kids out early with items like onesies, baby blankets, and diapers. And you and your little ones can become your favorite Princesses with costumes featuring your favorite characters. Whether it's Halloween or you just like to play dress-up, take a look at the costumes and accessories for all ages, ranging from newborn to adult.

Party supplies: When your little princess's birthday rolls around, create a themed celebration with Disney Princess party supplies. Just pick a theme, choose all of your supplies online or in-store, pick up a cake and you've successfully completed your party planning. Decorate with items like stickers, streamers, balloons, and other fun items featuring everyone from Jasmine to Belle. Guests can enjoy the cake and ice cream with Disney Princess cups, plates, bowls, napkins, and silverware. Afterward, the kids can take turns swinging at a Princess-themed piata. Finally, send your guests home with a Disney Princess goody bag filled with fun favors and treats.

Furniture and decor: Your child has the toys, the bike, and the outfit. Now, the ultimate Disney Princess fan should also have a place to keep it all. Shop for beds, bedding, and other gear fit for a princess. You'll find toddler beds, toy boxes, canopy beds, organizers, and more items to help turn your child's bedroom into the ultimate shrine to favorite characters. Going to sleep at night will be magical.

Books and movies: With all of the Disney Princess gear available, it's hard to remember that there's a story behind each unique character that makes her a strong female role model for kids. Share it with your kids through our selection of books and movies. Together, you can read picture books about your favorite Princesses, like Belle and Tiana, or plan a family movie night around films like Brave, Mulan, and Tangled. Look for collector's editions of your favorite Disney movies on DVD and Blu-ray.
