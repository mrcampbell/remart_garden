About Beverages - Walmart.com

Top tips on how to buy beverages

Whether it s a hot drink for breakfast, a refreshing soda for the kids or something stronger for adults, beverages come in endless options. We have you covered with delicious options for the entire family at Every Day Low Prices, so you can all enjoy your own favorite beverage. From fruit juices and soft drinks to spirits and wine, there's something for every occasion when you shop for beverages at Walmart.





Hot drinks

A cup of hot coffee in the morning, tea in the afternoon or hot cocoa at bedtime are life s simple pleasures for many. Explore our selection of hot beverages that add a refreshing touch to any of your daily breaks.





  • Coffee comes in multiple forms, with roasted beans and instant coffee being two of the most popular choices. Flavored coffee with hazelnut, Irish cream or vanilla can add an extra touch of interest to a cup, whereas decaffeinated coffee is perfect for those who like to enjoy their coffee in the evening as well.
  • Green, black, oolong and yellow teas all come from the same camellia sinensis plant. If you want something herbal, opt for mint, rooibos or chamomile teas that have a refreshing effect any time of day.
  • A mug of hot chocolate is tasty and soothing; it's a delicious treat for children and adults alike. Choose a cup of simple hot cocoa or go for a flavored one for a richer experience.

Soft drinks and fruit juices

From classics such as apple and orange juice to vegetable juices and exotic concoctions with pomegranate, coconut or pineapple, the selection of fruit juices at Walmart is huge. Opt for freshly squeezed juice for the least amount of processing, or go for a "not from concentrate" product to enjoy real fruit taste. Alternatively, have a look at the selection of soft drinks that come in handy for a party. From Cola drinks to cream soda and root beer, there's something refreshing for every palate.





Alcoholic drinks

Get ready to party with popular alcoholic beverages that come in a wide range of types, flavors and strengths. Our selection of adult drinks has everything from beers and wines to spirits and liqueurs.

Beer and cider: Get all the supplies you need for the big game with our large selection of beers and ciders. From lager beers and ales to fruit beers for those looking for a refreshing alternative, it's easy to find everyone s favorite drinks. Enjoy a cold cider on a summer day, or go for wheat beer if you want something with a distinctive taste.

Wine: Whether you're a wine lover or someone who s only drinking it occasionally, the selection of wines has something for every taste. Opt for a complex, rich red wine to go with your steak, or choose a bottle of white to pair with pasta and desserts. Get ready to celebrate a family event in style with a bottle of champagne.

Spirits: If you re looking for something stronger, have a look at our selection of spirits that includes vodka, whiskey, gin, rum, tequila and more. For something a bit more subtle, look for a spicy or fruity liqueur that goes well with a dessert such as a trifle or even with coffee.





Sports and energy drinks

People with an active lifestyle often need something to give them a boost so they can enjoy their activities to the fullest. With our selection of sports and energy drinks, you can remain in the game for as long as you want.





  • Sports drinks work better than water when you engage in strenuous activities because they help to replace the electrolytes you lose through sweating. Keep your body fluids well balanced and restore the carbohydrates you use during physical activities with sports drinks that are available in multiple flavors for all tastes.
  • Most energy drinks that are designed to enhance performance and improve concentration contain caffeine and plant extracts such as guarana, carbohydrates in the form of sugar, vitamins and amino acids such as taurine.
  • If you re looking for something without caffeine that can still boost your energy levels, choose nutritional drinks that come packed with vitamins to supplement your diet.
