Better Homes and Gardens

Better Homes and Gardens magazine is filled with fun decorating ideas, healthy recipes and garden tips. This is a must-read for anyone who likes to create things and who enjoys personalizing the home with decor items. You'll learn how to pick the right color for your bedroom walls, read about arranging flowers from your garden, find some helpful tips for throwing a successful party and discover some delicious recipes with seasonal ingredients. And that's just for starters.

Better Homes and Gardens is more than a magazine. It is also a line of products offered at Walmart. You can now shop for furniture, home decor items and even outdoor cooking appliances inspired by this publication. This line of products was made to help you create a welcoming home. Whether you need a few items to customize your bedroom, want to redo your bathroom or would like to create an outdoor living space where you can cook and relax, this product line has something for you.

Discover the Better Homes and Gardens line.

This original product line is all about style and comfort. You can shop for sleek and comfortable furniture designed for everyday living. You'll find decor items with bold patterns and graphics that truly stand out. These colorful products are going to help you create a home that matches your personality and your lifestyle.

Upgrade your bathroom.

Turn your bathroom into a place where you can truly relax and recharge your batteries by adding some plushy bath rugs and mats and by replacing your old shower curtain. Add a few bath accessories like toothbrush holders or lotion pumps for your beauty products. Don t forget to check out the bath towel selection so you can pamper yourself with a new towel set.

Get a good night s sleep.

Your bed is the central element of your bedroom. Shop for bedding sets with graphic and bold patterns to make a statement, or opt for a quilt, blanket or throw. Get matching sheets and pillow cases, and don t forget to add a few decorative pillows.

Shop for decor items.

The Better Homes and Gardens product line includes many decor items. You can easily find the right items by looking for a specific type of product, checking categories or browsing by collection. You can easily upgrade a room and make it look larger by adding light fixtures or a lamp. Candle holders are a great way to make any room feel more intimate, and rugs and decorative pillows make your bedroom or living room look more welcoming. Take a look at the selection of wall art to personalize any room.

Find sleek & comfy furniture.

From book cases to TV stands, Better Homes and Gardens furniture is designed to help you organize your home. Look for a new dresser for your clothes, display your favorite books with a bookcase or create your home office with a desk and a cube organizer. You'll also find kitchen and dining tables and chairs, living room furniture and kid s furniture.

Create your perfect kitchen.

Your kitchen is the room where you get to catch up with your loved ones over a delicious dinner after a long day. Turn this room into a comfortable nest with a new dining set. Save space by adding a cabinet or a counter organizer. If you like trying the recipes from the Better Homes and Gardens magazine, take a look at the cookware, bakeware and other kitchen tools. And if you love having guests over, you'll find some useful items in the dining and entertaining section such as dinnerware sets, trays and beverage dispensers.

Patio and gardening.

Take advantage of your garden or patio by upgrading this space with some furniture and decor items. An umbrella and an outdoor furniture set are all you need to enjoy a nice summer day. Outdoor lighting options like string lights, lanterns or spotlights allow you to enjoy your garden after dark. And if you love grilling, look into creating a small outdoor kitchen with a grill.

Organize your belongings.

Clutter can accumulate and make your home feel smaller than it actually is. Organize your belongings with the right storage solution. Storage bins and boxes are great for toys and books. Save space in your bathroom or laundry room with a new hamper. Closet and shoe organizers are a great addition to your bedroom if you feel that you can never find the right outfit and want your wardrobe to take up less space.

Shop for kids' room items.

A chest or some storage boxes help you save space in your child s room. A loft bed can also be a good option once your child is old enough. Make your child s room look more fun with bedding and decor items that reflect your son or daughter's personality. Shop for colorful curtains, add a comfortable area rug or get a comforter set in your child s favorite color. Upgrading your kid s room at Everyday Low Prices has never been easier.
