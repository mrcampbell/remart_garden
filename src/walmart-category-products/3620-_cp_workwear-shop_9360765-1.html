About Workwear Shop - Walmart.com

 Workwear and Work Boots 

At Walmart, you’ll find an extensive collection of innovative gear for can-do men and women. We offer a durable, functional selection of workwear and footwear to help keep you comfortable no matter what the workday throws at you. Find the perfect work jacket that’s water repellent, work pants made to move with you plus, the toughest boots for all terrains and so much more from your favorite brands including  Dickies, Gildan, Hanes, Big Smith, Wrangler, Skechers Work, Timberland PRO, Herman Survivors, Brahma and more! 

The Right Workwear. With the right workwear, you’ll get your projects done faster and safer. Walmart’s innovative products
 can help keep you and your teams warm in the winter, cool in the summer and help increase performance across job roles.

Dress for the job with these 8 workwear essentials, available in sizes up to 3XL. 

  1. Work coveralls, shirts, pants, and vests from Dickies, Big Smith, Wrangler, George and more withstand daily use and offer great comfort and mobility.
  2. Work boots with steel toes, slip-resistant soles and waterproof uppers from Pro, Skechers Work, Herman Survivors, Brahma and more stand the test of time and keep your feet comfortable, dry and supported throughout the day.
  3. High-visibility jackets, coats, pants, and shirts from Walls, RK Safety, North 15 and more use bright colors and reflective details to keep workers visible and safer in low-light conditions. 
  1. Water-repellent jackets, pants and shirts from Bellingham, Propper, Khall and more will help keep you dry and warm in the great outdoors
  2. Cold-weather headwear, jackets, coats, sweatshirts, and foot and hand warmers from Dickies, Smith’s Workwear, Allegro and more retain heat and provide extra warmth in cold work environments.
  3. Cooling vests and jackets from Techniche, Pyramex, Hyperkewl and more keep you cool in hot work environments. 
  1. Protective chaps and cut-resistant sleeves shield legs and arms against cuts and abrasions. 
  1. Lab coats and scrubs provide work clothing that's well-suited to medical settings.

At Walmart, we carry the workwear essentials you need at everyday low prices—we’re here to outfit everyone from the tradesmen and builders who need safety boots and thick gloves to our school crossing guards who require a high-visibility jacket and more…we’ve got you covered!
