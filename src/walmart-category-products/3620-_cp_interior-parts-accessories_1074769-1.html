About Interior Parts & Accessories - Walmart.com

Sprucing Up Your Automotive Interior

Our cars have become an integral part of our everyday lives. From commuting to and from work, taking kids to school or practice, shopping and running errands, to escaping on a road trip adventure, Americans collectively spent 70 billion hours behind the wheel over the course of a year. Why not make the journey just as exciting as the destination by making your car your own, be that comfier or cooler, with Walmart.com and our selection of Interior Accessories such as Seat Covers, Consoles & Organizers, Sun Shades & Visors, Air Fresheners, Floor Mats and Steering Wheel Covers, and many other products at our Every Day Low Price value.

Car Seat Covers are a quick and affordable way to upgrade your car's interior. Car Seat Covers are a stylish and customizable way to help protect your car's original upholstery (helping to preserve your vehicle's resale value), add an extra layer of comfort and cushioning to your seats, or to simply spice up your ride with stylish colors, patterns, and materials. If you have pets and want to keep your seats clean, consider a bench seat cover, it's designed to be used on the back seats without leaving any gaps, so cleaning up after Fido is fast and easy. Steering wheel covers and floor mats are another great way to further customize your car by protecting your car's original upholstery and making clean up that much easier too.

Consoles & Organizers are a perfect way to de-clutter your car and keep it that way too. We carry hundreds of solutions for every space in your car including Consoles, Back-Seat Organizers, Cup Holders, Trunk Organizers, Trash Cans and much more.

Protect your car from heat, snow, UV radiation, and glare with our many Sun Protection options, including Windshield Protection, Visors, Show Covers, Sun Shades.

From Floral to Sweet, Woody & Earthy to Warm & Spicy, or to classic Fresh, there are countless Air Freshener options to choose from for your car. Whether you're a fan of the modern, vent-mounted air fresheners or the classic Little Tree hanging from your rear-view mirror, you'll find an assortment of refreshing scents to stay relaxed and comfortable on the road.
