Jewelry & Watches

Get ready to wow them. Shop Walmarts sensational selection of fine and fashion jewelry, pluswatches for him and her. Discover stunning earrings, bracelets, rings, and more for yourself, gifts for birthdays and anniversarieseven engagement rings for that special day. With so many styles and types of jewelry, were here to help you choose the perfect piece, its your time to shine.
What types of necklaces does Walmart have? Walmart features a great collection of stunning diamond necklaces, gemstone necklaces, chains and chokers, mens jewelry, baby jewelrytheres something for everyone. Keep on trend with personalized necklaces, fashion pendants, and jewelry sets, too!

Types of earring styles. From studs to hoops and chandelier earrings, weve got the 411 on what you need at the prices you want. Your ears arent pierced? No problem, weve got clip-on earrings, too.

  1. Hoop earrings: An everyday favorite. Choose from charm hoops, multi-hoops and inside-out hoops, all in 10-14K gold or sterling silver and with or without diamonds and gemstones.
  2. Drop earrings: Features an ornament or gemstone that dangles. Go short or extravagantly long for instant drama.
  3. Stud earrings: Feature a gemstone or ornament on a post. Very popular is solitaire diamond studs.
  4. Chandelier earrings: A type of drop earrings that resembles the shape of a chandelier light fixture, which they are named after. Usually contains gemstone for added elegance.
  5. Crawler earrings: Sometimes called climber earrings start at the bottom of your ear and crawl up the lobe, making a big statement.
  6. Jacket earrings: Add impact to smaller earrings like studs. Feature a hole in the center so the post of an existing earring can slip through.
  7. Leverback earrings: Provide security to post earrings since the lever holds the earring in place.
  8. Omega back earrings: Feature a hinge that is attached to a clip that flip back; usually in the shape of the Greek letter Omega.

What are the most popular bracelets? The most popular bracelets for women arebangle bracelets, cuff bracelets, chain & link bracelets, beaded bracelets, charm bracelets,and more. Leather bracelets and anchor bracelets make a great choice for men. Get ready for the ultimate arm candyWalmart has a sensational collection of over 1,000 bracelets for men and women.

Shopping for an Engagement Ring? Shopping for an engagement ring is one of the biggest purchased youll ever make. Heres everything you need to know to get the perfect ring at Walmart that youll treasure forever:

  • Know the 4 Cs: Cut, carat, clarity, and color
  • Pick your shape: Round, oval, emerald, princess, marquise, or pear shape
  • Have a budget in mind.

Plus, Walmart has promise rings, bridal sets, wedding bands for him and her, anniversary rings, and much more to help you celebrate many special moments!

How do I pick the perfect watch? If the circumference of your wrist is 6 to 7 inches, you want to go with a watch that has case diameter of 38-42 mm wide. If your wrist is larger than 7 inches, then you can go with cases that are 44-46 mm wide.

Visit Walmarts Watch Shop. From cutting-edge features to classic timepieces, Walmart help you keep time in style with a great assortment of smart watches, kids character watches, and some trendy watches for him and her from the brands you love: Fossil, Bulova, Relic by Fossil, Times, Kenneth Cole, Armitron, and more!

How to Organize Jewelry. Keep your jewelry organized, untangled, and within reach with a handy jewelry box, a jewelry organizer with divided section, in a jewelry armoire, jewelry tree, and a ring dish. Walmart also offers cleaning solutions and jewelry repair kits to keep your jewelry looking its best.
