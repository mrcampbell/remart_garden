Baby Gift Guide

Give parents gifts they'll reach for as the days get longer and the weather gets warmer. From infant clothing and warm-weather travel gear to diapers and wipes, there's something for everyone with a little one at home. Whether you're shopping for yourself, a friend's child, or a new parent, at Walmart, you can find deals on all things baby all season long.

Not sure what to get the little one on your shopping list? Give them something that will spark their imagination. Activity saucers are a great way to keep a little one occupied and can get them moving their legs before they're ready to walk on their own. While in an activity center, babies can develop their coordination and muscle strength and have freedom of movement while still being comfortably supported. Many exersaucers have toys on both the top and bottom to help baby develop hand-eye coordination as well as sensory skills. They're also a great place for your baby to hang out while you're outside enjoying the warm sunshine. Looking for a gift for a toddler but don't know where to start? Browse baby gifts by age so you can find something age-appropriate that they'll love.

If you know someone who is expecting their bundle of joy to arrive during the spring or summer season, consider a great gift they'll use for years, like a car seat or a crib. If you don't know what they already have or what they still need, get them a gift card so they can buy what they really want. Are you expecting but didn't get everything on your wish list? Now is the perfect time to stock up with great deals on the essentials, like breast pumps and nursing supplies, bottle warmers and cleaners, lightweight blankets and breathable onesies to keep cool, and strollers. Need a gift that's within your budget? Shop by price to find great gifts for under $10, under $25, under $50, and over $50.

From toddler beds to baby monitors, Walmart has great gifts for babies and parents alike.
