Candles & Home Fragrance

Candles don't just make your house smell good. They can also enhance your decor and add a warming glow to your space. From Yankee Candle and WoodWick to Mainstays and Better Homes & Gardens, you'll be able to find the perfect candles and home fragrances all at everyday low prices at Walmart.

How can I make my whole house smell good?

Candles and wax warmers are a great way to make your house smell fresh. Place pillar and three-wick candles in spaces that are most frequented, like on your coffee table, on your office desk, or on your nightstand next to your bed. Alternate scents if you're going to be burning your candles daily, so you don't get used to the scent. If you have young children or pets, wax warmers are a good alternative if you don't want an open flame in your house. Even if you don't have kids or pets, never leave a burning candle unattended or near curtains or other fabrics.

What is the best candle to buy?

When choosing the best candle, it's important to consider the type of candle, the scent, and the shape. Traditional candles are either made from soy or beeswax. Soy candles are vegan, made from natural materials, and are usually long-burning. Beeswax candles tend to smell sweeter and have a higher burning temperature. If you like the look of candles but don't necessarily want the scent, LED candles are a great option. When selecting a scent, think about what smells you like. Warm & spicy and woody & earthy scents work well in the fall, while fruity and floral scents are perfect for springtime. A pillar or votive candle in a candle holder work well indoors, while lanterns can add a decorative touch to your backyard or porch.
