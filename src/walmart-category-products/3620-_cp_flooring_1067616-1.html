About Flooring - Walmart.com

Flooring

Transform your home from the ground up with Walmart's selection of flooring options. There are many ways to maximize the potential of your home with flooring, whether it's with lamination or shims and spacers. Here are few things to keep in mind when shopping around so you can find the right flooring for your home.

Style and function

Once you've figured out the basic square footage of your home (or the area where you plan to install new flooring), the next thing you'll want to consider is style and function. Wood flooring, for example, has a natural and inviting presence that is unrivaled by its competition with its classic design; the tradeoff is that it is also known to dent, wear and show discoloration more easily. More durable materials like linoleum and ceramic are better at combating these issues. Despite the higher price tag, these materials are easier to repair and replace, and tend to offer better value long-term.

Get the story right

Choosing the right color scheme or finish can do wonders for your home, as well as choosing flooring that provides the best environment for family and guests. If you're on a budget and want something that looks good without breaking the bank, then try vinyl flooring. With shock-resistant and water proof features, it's an ideal choice for kitchens or bathrooms. Floor carpeting is another great option. A must-have for homes with children, this type of flooring is soundproof and can cover uneven subfloors and insulation, providing a safe space for kids of all ages to play and lounge in.

No professional work? No problem

For those who'd rather take matters into their own hands, Walmart offers a great selection of DIY materials and tools so you save more time and money. With options like "peel and stick" and "click and lock," transforming your home has never been more easy and affordable.
