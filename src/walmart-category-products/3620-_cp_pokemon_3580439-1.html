About Pokemon - Walmart.com

Pokemon

Is your dream to become the greatest Pokemon master of all time? In the Pokemon universe, you can collect and train wild Pocket Monsters by catching them in Pokeballs, small balls used by Pokemon Trainers to catch and store Pokemon. In some of the Pokemon universes, you can battle your Pokemon to make them bigger and stronger. In other universes, the creatures don't battle and are just kept in a collection. Catch them all, and you'll be a Pokemon master.

Pokemon was once originally a series of video games, but over the years has evolved into a franchise of games, a television series, movies, toys, and more. Whether your favorite Pokemon universe was the animated series, the video games, or just the stuffed plushes and toys, you'll be able to find everything you need to outfit your fandom at Every Day Low Prices.

Gotta catch 'em all

No matter if you're shopping for yourself or for a gift for the Pokemon fan in your life, you can find a wide variety of Pokemon items at Walmart. Expand your collection with a pack of trading cards or with a game for the Nintendo Switch. Looking for something your little one will love? Check out the toys, stuffed animals, and books category for a gift that will let their imagination run wild. You can also decorate their bedroom and bathroom with posters, bedding, rugs, and shower curtains featuring their favorite Pokemon.

Throwing a Pokemon-themed party? Shop our selection of party supplies for balloons, plates, decorations, and greeting cards to make it extra special. Don't forget to check out the costume and clothing categories to dress for the occasion. You can also complete your look with watches, rings, necklaces, and other Pokemon accessories.

I choose you, Pikachu!

Not sure where to start? Only looking for toys or shirts of your favorite Pokemon? Shop our collection of Pokemon items by character. You can browse different items by selecting the Pikachu, Eevee, Charizard, Charmander, Squirtle, Bulbasaur, Jigglypuff, Greninja, or Psyduck categories.

From DVD collector sets to vinyl figures and everything in between, you can find a wide assortment of Pokemon items at Walmart, so you can Save Money. Live Better.
