The Character Shop at Walmart 

 Quick. Click and pick their favorite characters! Shop Walmart’s Character Shop and find all of their favorite characters from superheroes to TV characters, movie favorites and more! Scoop up your kids’ favorites including: Trolls, Frozen 2, Jojo Siwa, Minnie Mouse, Mickey Mouse, Baby Shark, Scoob!, Disney Princesses, Marvel Avengers, L.O.L. Surprise, SpongeBob, Star Wars and so much more. Find girls’ and boys’ t-shirts, sweatshirts, costumes, dress-up and more, all at amazing everyday low prices! Let’s get into character… 

Surprise your little or big girl with character clothing including playful tees with fun logos, tulle trim skirts, shiny jackets and more to showcase her love of these adorable characters. Discover all-time favorites like Disney princesses, Minnie Mouse, Jojo Siwa, Frozen 2 and so much more! Her favorite characters can be found on long-sleeve tees, hoodies, dresses activewear sets, pajamas and more! Don’t forget essentials like socks, underwear, swimwear, and even umbrellas to complete her wardrobe. 

Every boy loves a  superhero! Find boys’ clothing that feature Superman, Batman, and countless others. Plus, favorite characters from Star Wars, Disney, SpongeBob, Fortnite and more! Stock up on a few graphic tees, but don’t forget those special gifts like a superhero cape t-shirt, designed with a removable cape that makes playtime more fun than ever. Try a pair of jeans with a fun character patch or a two-piece set with matching t-shirt and pants. Finish with plenty of underwear and pajamas with his favorite characters. 

Any way you go back, we’ve got your back! Back-to-School has never been more exciting—get carried away and scoop up character-inspired lunch boxes, backpacks, shoes and sneakers made to make the day just a little more fun! 

Visit the Character Shop today for everything you could want for fans of all ages…now at Walmart and at everyday low prices!
