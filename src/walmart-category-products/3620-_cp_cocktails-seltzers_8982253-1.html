Do hard seltzers have fewer carbs than beer?

Most brands of hard seltzer have about two grams of carbohydrates. Standard beers usually have between 10 and 15 grams of carbs due to their high starch content. Some brewers are releasing beers with a lower carb content that are more comparable to hard seltzers. Low-carb beers contain special enzymes that break down the carbs during the brewing process.

Are hard seltzers a good choice if I'm watching my weight?

Hard seltzers aren't calorie-free like non-alcoholic seltzers are. If you're trying to lose or maintain your weight, hard seltzers are lower in calories and sugar than most other alcoholic cocktails. Many hard seltzers are low in sugar, and a few even have zero grams of sugar, so read the nutrition information carefully.

Which spirits mix well with hard seltzers?

Pair a citrus flavored hard seltzer with a clear spirit like vodka or gin. Make a wine spritzer by mixing a hard seltzer with white wine.
