Walmart Specialty Pharmacy

Walmart Specialty Pharmacy offers personalized, affordable care. If you need help managing your chronic illness, we can give you the care you need without breaking the bank. Read on to see if Walmart Specialty Pharmacy is right for you.

What's the difference between a specialty pharmacy and a regular pharmacy?

If you needed some allergy medication, you'd probably head down to your local pharmacy. But if you deal with long-term illnesses that require close management with a pharmacist, you'd need to find a specialty pharmacy. While regular pharmacies deal with over-the-counter medications, vaccinations, and common medications, specialty pharmacies concentrate on treating long-term conditions that may be more difficult to manage. Specialty pharmacies provide storage, handling, and administration of medications used to treat chronic, complex, or rare diseases. If your local pharmacy doesn't have the medication you need to manage your health, check to see if Walmart Specialty Pharmacy can help.

Is my medication available at Walmart Specialty Pharmacy?

Walmart Specialty Pharmacy makes getting the care you need easy. If you suffer from conditions like acromegaly, anemia, arrhythmia, cancer, cholesterol, Crohn's disease, cystic fibrosis, growth hormones, hemophilia, hepatitis, HIV/AIDS, infertility, IVIG, multiple sclerosis, neutropenia, osteoporosis, pulmonary arterial hypertension, rheumatoid arthritis, or transplant, we might be able to help.
