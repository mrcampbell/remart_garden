About Arts, Crafts & Sewing - Walmart.com

Arts, Crafts, and Sewing

Cure your creative cravings with a fun weekend project with the kids or a quick DIY adventure with a friend. Whether you're creating fashionable apparel or decal for your home, Walmart.com offers a wide variety of arts and crafts or sewing items.

Channeling creativity in young kids can be a joy or a challenge. Creating weekend projects like a wind chime for the front porch or button art for the refrigerator, arts and crafts can be a fun way to get your kids off of their smart devices or off the TV and get in touch with their creative side. Items like colored construction paper and assorted markers, pencils, crayons and pens can be used to make a wide range of creative projects. Jewelry, pottery, and model sets are great for creating functioning and wearable art like bracelets and pots for the plants you've been wanting to pot. Keeping their creative space clean is a breeze with adjustable hobby tables that can be folded and stored away or craft amours that are perfect for providing a place to work and that also provides ample storage for all of their art supplies and tools.

If you're trying your hand at sewing, whether to make clothing or home decor items like pillowcases or a tablecloth, items like a sewing machine or a hot glue gun can assist you in getting your DIY project started. Sewing machines are available in styles ranging from thread-less and single-stitch machines to heavy-duty sewing and embroidery machines. Die cut machines are a quick and efficient way to bring your DIY project to life using wireless configuration to cut shapes and patterns into a variety of fabrics including leather and other thick fabrics. Adding fun details like a crochet trim to your throw pillow or knitted shoulder straps onto your favorite wallet to transform it into your favorite crossbody bag can be easy with a weaving loop or a variety pack of crochet needles.

Using a sewing or craft table is a great way to keep your arts and crafts tools and products organized and in one place. They're available in different sizes and shapes, some with a chair or workbench, others with cabinets and pull-out drawers. They're also useful for providing a clean and level working station for creating summer scrapbooks or family photo albums. Storing your many craft tools and art supplies doesn't have to be a nightmare, using items like craft boxes or utility-style carts can make keeping your workspace clean and organized. Craft boxes are available in transparent and solid colors and offer different compartment sizes and lift-away trays and can be set on top of a counter or underneath a desk or workstation. Utility carts are also available in assorted colors, size, and shapes and can be stored in a closet or empty corner in your work area to maximize space.

Whether you're passionate about art, looking to add a little personality to your space, or simply want to find a project to get your hands dirty, Walmart.com has everything you need to let your creativity fly.
