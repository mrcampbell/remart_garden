About Electric Transportation - Walmart.com

Electric Bikes

Put some power behind your pedals. Electric bicycles, or e-bikes, feature a battery and a motor to help you pedal faster or with less effort. Whether you're riding on pavement to get to work or on rocky terrain down a mountain, Walmart.com has a wide variety of e-bikes from brands like Schwinn, Nakto, Ancheer, and more to suit your riding style at everyday low prices.

What are some different types of e-bikes?

  • Hub-drive motors: Generally the most popular type, hub-driven electric bikes feature the motor in the center of either the front or back wheel. They have more power than mid-drive bikes, and they also allow you to cruise without pedaling by engaging the throttle-only option. They need less maintenance and are usually cheaper, so if you're looking for a good deal that doesn't require a lot of upkeep, a hub-drive e-bike is a good option.
  • Mid-drive motors: Instead of being placed on the wheel, mid-drive e-bikes have the motor closer to the center of the bike, near the pedals. In addition to being smaller and lighter than hub-driven bikes, mid-drive e-bikes offer a smoother ride and can start from a full stop, even on steep terrain.
  • Electric mountain bikes: These sturdy bikes, also called eMTBs, are best for rough terrain and off-road trails. They usually have more upright handlebars for easy handling over mud and rocks, and wide tires to help absorb shock from landing jumps.
  • Electric road bikes: Electric road bikes are great for commuters or long-distance riders. Unlike mountain bikes, they have narrow tires that help provide a smooth ride on flat pavement. They also usually feature a lighter frame and a higher seat position to make riding more comfortable and pedal assist to help you move faster and easier.
  • Electric hybrid bikes: An electric hybrid bike is a combination of a mountain bike and a road bike. They're a great all-purpose bike because they combine the lightweight frame of road bike with the wide tires and higher clearance of a mountain bike.
  • Electric cruiser bikes: An electric cruiser is a great choice if you're a leisure rider and love the styling of a vintage bicycle. They come in an assortment of different colors and can have attachments like baskets for holding cargo.
  • Electric bike conversion kits: Build your own dream e-bike with an electric bike conversion kit. If you're looking for something specific or already have a bike that you love, conversion kits are a cost-effective and simple way to make it into an e-bike.

From folding and commuter bikes to helmets and accessories, you can find everything you need to get out and ride so you can save money and live better at Walmart.
