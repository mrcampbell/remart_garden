About Digestive Health - Walmart.com

What can probiotics do for me?

Probiotics help balance the amount of good and bad bacteria in your body, which can help you with digestion. Taking probiotics may help prevent or stop diarrhea, can help keep allergies at bay, and might boost your immune system. Some studies have shown that they may help with weight loss, heart health, and depression as well. Always consult with your doctor before adding probiotics to your daily routine.

What should I take for acid reflux?

You have many over-the-counter options when it comes to acid reflux and heartburn. For quick relief of acid reflux that isn't reoccurring, consider taking antacids. Histamine-2 (H2) blockers work to prevent heartburn and can last for hours. Proton pump inhibitors (PPIs) work for frequent heartburn, but they do not work immediately. You must take them for a few days before they take effect. Always read the label and follow directions for any over-the-counter medication you take and consult with your doctor before you take new medications.

What can I give my child for an upset stomach?

When your child has an upset stomach, look for over-the-counter medications geared specifically towards children. Products like probiotics, gas drops, antacids, laxatives, and diarrhea medication may offer temporary relief. Be sure to read the label to ensure the product is suitable for your child's age. You should also consider talking to your pediatrician or a certified Walmart pharmacist before giving your child medication.
