About Gap Home at Walmart - Walmart.com

Gap Home at Walmart

Make your home your sanctuary with some fresh new decor. Elevate your home decor with the help of Gap Home, Gap's first-ever home collection. Discover timeless American style, quality designs, and go-to essentials that instantly refresh a room with Gap Home, exclusively at Walmart. Available online only.

Bath and bedding

The closest you'll get to feeling like you're sleeping on a cloud. Upgrade your bedding with breathable, comfortable sleep sets featuring sheets and pillowcases made with the planet in mind. Rest and relaxation don't have to stop at your bedroom door. Refresh your bath with minimalistic toothbrush holders, soap dispensers, and matching bathmats. Whether you're shopping for sculpted bath towels, sleek accessories, or accent pillows for your bed, you'll be able to find items made with organic cotton and recycled materials at everyday low prices.

Everyday essentials

From subtle prints and neutral shades to bold hues and patterns that pop, Gap Home has pieces that will make your space feel like home. Shop the selection of breezy curtains, sophisticated dinnerware, and stackable stoneware cups. Looking for something specific? Use the shop by category feature to fill your home with inspired finds for daily life.
