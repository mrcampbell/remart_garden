What are some alternatives to grilling burgers and hotdogs?

Grill seafood on skewers. Layer shrimp, peppers, and onions and baste with your favorite dressing. Try grilling wings and corn on the cob. Romaine lettuce can be grilled by cutting the head of lettuce in half and brushing with oil. Grill on both sides for a few minutes until you see the grill marks on the lettuce.

What labels indicate a Walmart brand?

Walmart brands include Great Value, Marketside and Sam's Choice.

How do you make dessert kabobs?

Use firm fruits like strawberries, bananas, or pineapple. Stack on skewers with marshmallows, chunks of brownies, or gummy candy. Add chocolate drizzle and sprinkles. Refrigerate until you are ready to serve.
