What does Walmart Gift eligible mean?

Certain products on Walmart.com qualify as Gift eligible, which means we will send the recipient an email with a personalized message from you and a digital receipt. The receipt will not include pricing, but they can use it if they want to return or exchange your gift. To find Gift eligible items, select "Gift eligible" under the Gifting category on the left side of a page of products. When the page refreshes, all products displayed will be Gift eligible. When you check out, you'll be able to designate the product as a gift, write your message, and add the recipient's email address.

How do I wash my child's backpack?

Most backpacks made for kids can be washed in a washing machine, but it's best to use the gentle cycle. Turn the backpack inside out and make sure any crumbs and other items are out. Put the bag in a mesh laundry bag or pillow case tied at the top to keep straps from getting caught in the machine and hardware from banging on the sides. Use cold or warm water and mild detergent. Once the backpack is washed, turn it right side out and stuff it with towels to keep its shape while it dries. Set the pack on a waterproof surface to dry.

Can I wash a plush toy in a washing machine?

Unless the plush toy is older and worn, you can usually wash a plush toy in the gentle cycle of your washing machine without damaging it. It's best to add other clothes or towels to the cycle to keep the toys from catching on the agitator. Putting plushies in a pillowcase will also help protect buttons, eyes, and other hard objects.
