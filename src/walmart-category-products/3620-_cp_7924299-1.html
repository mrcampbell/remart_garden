Need a new scent? How do you choose a fragrance?  Choosing a fragrance for yourself or a gift can be challenging.  At Walmart, we have the best fragrances. Here’s a few shopping tips for finding your signature scent:

Shop by Scent. Identify the scents you love with these 6 fragrance families:

  1. Fresh: discover marine notes like seaweed and driftwood
  2. Warm & Spicy: enjoy warm notes like cinnamon, pink pepper and clover
  3. Woody & Earthy: find common notes like sandalwood or patchouli
  4. Floral: nature’s own beauties like roses and jasmine
  5. Sweet: irresistible notes like chocolate, vanilla and caramel
  6. Citrus: light notes like lemon, citrus and grapefruit 

Know the Concentration. Choose the level of concentration based on how powerful you want your fragrance to be. Here are the five classifications:

  1. Eau Frache. The lightest formula. Perfect for those who like to apply their scent generously.
  2. Eau de Cologne. A lighter concentration that is subtle and refreshing
  3. Eau de Toilette. A moderate concentration, distinct and alluring
  4. Eau de Parfum. Lighter than perfume, yet fragrant enough to las the day
  5. The purest and most powerful blend.
