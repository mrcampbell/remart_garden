About Barbie - Walmart.com

Spark your Child's Imagination with Barbie

Your child can be anything with Barbie. Let their imagination run wild. Whether they want to be a teacher, astronaut, dancer, or doctor, the possibilities are endless and Barbie can help them explore through pretend play. The childhood classic doll is so much more than a toy. From clothing and accessories for dolls and people to home goods, we have everything your little one needs to dream big with Barbie.

Toys

A Barbie is so much more than a doll. Your child can pretend they're on an epic road trip with friends while Barbie drives a vehicle. Or, they might imagine that they drive a cool convertible to their very own castle or mansion. If you're looking for a gift for the child in your life, a playset is a great way to go, especially because it includes so many toys in one. The small details in playsets make it the perfect gift for a child with a big imagination. From kitchen accessories to doctor tools, a playset has what a kid needs to let their imagination develop freely. If you're shopping for a child who has siblings, look for Barbie toys and playsets with complete 360-degree play. This means the sides of the structures are open so the kids can all get in on the action together.

While the Barbies you might've enjoyed when you were young had their own unique looks, 21st century Barbie is available in different body types, including curvy and tall. She's available with an array of skin tones, eye colors, hair colors, and hairstyles, too. Today's Barbies are realistic and designed to show children the beauty of diversity. From rock star glam to sporty, there are countless outfits for kids to choose for their Barbies (and Kens, too).

A Barbie with accessories is a great way to introduce children to these beloved dolls. Look for a set that inspires your little one to think big. Whether they want to travel to space, become president of the United States, or inspire others as a teacher, there's a Barbie for them.

Apparel

Every Barbie fan needs clothes to show off their favorite toy. Your youngster can dress up in two-piece short sets, T-shirts, socks, dresses, pajamas, nightgowns, and sunglasses in a variety of sizes. Orders over $35 qualify for free delivery. And if you pick up the wrong size, it's no problem. With hassle-free returns, you have nothing to worry about.

Party supplies

Throwing a Barbie-themed party? We make it easy to give your child a party they'll never forget. Choose tableware, party decorations, favors, baking supplies, and gift wrap, all sporting cool Barbie themes. Start your party planning by checking out tableware. Here you'll find plates, cups, napkins, and tablecloths. Next, it's time to decorate. Balloons are a great start, as is a table-decorating kit. For some added fun, pick up a scene setter. This is a large, two-piece poster you can hang on a wall that your child can take pictures in front of with their party guests. Instead of a cake, how about cupcakes? Barbie-themed rings double as cupcake toppers for guests to take home. And before friends leave, make sure they're toting goody bags brimming with Barbie accessories.

Home decor

Show off a love of Barbie with home decor for your child's bedroom. Want to go all out? A bed in a bag is the perfect choice. These sets make it easy to deck out an entire bed with coordinated linens. Look for a set that comes with a comforter, fitted sheet, flat sheet, and pillowcases. As a gift for a Barbie fan, a Barbie throw blanket is great for the bedroom, watching a movie on the couch, or making a blanket fort. If you need a gift fast, many of our Barbie home decor items are available with TwoDay Delivery.

Help from the Walmart App

One of the easiest ways to shop? With the Walmart App. It's free to download and makes shopping easy and fun. When you're shopping on the app, choose store pickup at checkout. We'll notify you as soon as your order is ready, and you can collect your items from your local Walmart. You can also use Walmart Pay right from your device to streamline the shopping process. Open the app when you're in our store and type in your PIN. Hold your phone over the code to scan and you're done. The payment card that you've securely saved to the app will be charged and an e-receipt is stored in your app. If you have to make a return, you can easily access your receipt right on the app. You can also enjoy these great benefits with the app:

  • Make a shopping list and get store information right from your device
  • Reorder everyday items like diapers, toilet paper, or other essentials
  • Securely send money to over 200 countries
  • Refill or transfer prescriptions and locate health services
