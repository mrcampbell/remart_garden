Clean Beauty at Walmart

What is Clean Beauty at Walmart?

Clean Beauty at Walmart is an online shop that helps our customers find beauty products that are made without ingredients they may not want. All featured beauty products are made without the ingredients included on Walmart’s Made Without List (MWL).* *Walmart’s Clean Beauty program does not guarantee that a product is “100% free from” ingredients on Walmart’s WML. Trace contaminants may exist.

What is Walmart’s Made Without List?

Walmart’s Made Without List (MWL) identifies chemicals that are not intentionally added to products in Walmart’s Clean Beauty program. This list includes several chemical classes such as Formaldehyde Releasers, Parabens, Phthalates and Per- and Polyfluorinated Substances. Currently, there are over 1200 ingredients on the MWL. Select “Download the list” for a full list of ingredients.

How was the Made Without List Developed?

Walmart’s Made Without List (MWL) was inspired by industry “clean” standards. To develop the list, Walmart reviewed state and federal regulations, consulted suppliers, and gathered input from authoritative experts such as the global safety science company, Underwriter Laboratories, and the environmental non-profit, Environmental Defense Fund. Walmart will evaluate the MWL annually, and may periodically revise the list to keep up with industry standards and customer expectations.
