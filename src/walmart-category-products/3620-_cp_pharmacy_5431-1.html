About Pharmacy | Online Rx Refills - Walmart.com

Walmart Pharmacy

Whether you're looking for online prescription refills or affordable immunizations, Walmart is your one-stop-shop for health and wellness for the whole family.

Can I get my prescription medication from Walmart Pharmacy?

Yes. Walmart Pharmacy offers an easy prescription experience, from medication refills with just a few clicks to low-cost generic meds starting at just $4. You can also transfer your prescription online quickly and easily. Just click "start a transfer" to create an account. Walmart's Specialty Pharmacy can also make it easy for you to get the medications you need at an affordable price. Check out our key health resources and more wellness services to learn more about selecting an insurance that's right for you, get your questions about Medicare answered, connect with a pharmacist for medication management, and information on pet meds for everyday low prices on medications for your furry friends.

What other services does Walmart Pharmacy offer?

In addition to getting your prescription medications, Walmart Pharmacy also offers affordable immunizations to help protect you from the flu, COVID-19, shingles, and more. Just click "schedule now" to find an appointment and Walmart Pharmacy location that's convenient for you. There's also no-cost interpretation with over 200 languages available for those who need an interpreter.
