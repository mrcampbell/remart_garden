Stay Connected and Entertained with iPads and Tablets

Tablets are wonderful, portable, multipurpose computers with a wide range of functions for work and play, so it's no wonder why they continue to grow in popularity. Whether you need to get some assignments done on the go, watch movies during a flight, or enjoy some portable app gaming, Walmart offers a variety of iPads and tablet PCs at everyday low prices to suit your needs. Before you make your selection, why not learn a bit more about what these exceptional electronics can do?

iPads

The popular Apple iPad is available in several different models, so you can choose the one that best meets your needs.

Upgrade your technology with the Apple iPad Pro. This tablet's anti-reflective screen coating makes it easy on the eyes--literally. It features a powerful Apple A12Z Bionic chip with 64-bit desktop-class architecture for super-fast movement in and out of your favorite apps. Four speakers drive the internal audio, and the iPad Pro features advanced iSight and FaceTime HD cameras for easy, clear chatting. It also has Wi-Fi and LTE connectivity to maintain your access on the go. iCloud allows you to conveniently store all your documents and information and retrieve them with a few easy swipes of the screen. Plus, the breakthrough face detection adds an extra measure of security. This retina display iPad provides 10 hours of battery life.

With its thin, light design, it's incredibly convenient to transport in your backpack, purse, or briefcase. It also includes great apps for productivity and creativity, as well as many more that are available in the online App Store. With powerful features like multitasking, Apple iPad Pro can do things no other iPad has done before, helping you to simplify and streamline your life. The iPad Pro comes in 11-inch and 12.9-inch retina display sizes and is available in 32-, 128- and 256-gigabyte models. There are two colors to choose from: space gray and silver.

The standard iPad is both powerful and portable with a beautiful 10.2-inch retina display in a thin, durable, aluminum design that weighs only a pound. This iPad puts incredible capabilities in your hands with a powerful A12 chip plus an 8-megapixel camera, FaceTime HD camera, Touch ID, Apple Pay, Wi-Fi, all-day battery life and over a million apps on the App Store. It's available in 32- and 128-gigabyte models, and there are three colors to choose from: space gray, gold, and silver.

Finally, there's the iPad mini 5. This is a terrific option if you'll be using your iPad on the go a lot. This little tablet may be small, but it's plenty powerful. The iPad mini 5 puts everything you love about your iPad into an even sleeker, more portable design. You can enjoy FaceTime calls with friends or get work done wherever and whenever you like. An A12 Bionic chip provides fast processing along with second-generation 64-bit desktop-class architecture that can handle even your most demanding apps. Whether you're editing a video, browsing photos, or doing both at the same time, your experience will be smooth and seamless. The mini also connects to fast Wi-Fi and cellular networks, so you can download content, browse the web and share documents from virtually anywhere. It's available in a 64- or 256-gigabyte model with three colors to choose from: space gray, gold, and silver.

There are also plenty of other iPads to choose from. You can save money with new and refurbished models like iPad 2, iPad Air, iPad mini 2, and many others.

More tablets

If you're looking for other tablet PCs, you'll find plenty of choices, including highly economical ones.

Look for a tablet with at least 8 gigabytes of capacity and get one with an available SD slot if you want to expand your storage options later on. Also, pay attention to speed: 1.5 gigahertz or more is best if you'll be running multiple apps at the same time.

One popular tablet is the Microsoft Surface Pro 7. You can go from an ultraportable tablet to a complete laptop in a snap (literally) wherever you happen to be thanks to the folding keyboard. The Surface Pro features a 12.3-inch display and runs up to 11 hours on a full charge. It's loaded with features like a back 8-megapixel webcam and front 5-megapixel HD webcam with microphone and auto-focus. It also offers a USB 3.0 port, headset jack, additional memory via a microSD card reader, stereo speakers with Dolby audio, and more.
