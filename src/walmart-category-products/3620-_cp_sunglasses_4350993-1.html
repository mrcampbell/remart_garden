About Sunglasses - Walmart.com

What does polarized mean?

Polarized is another way to say anti-reflective, and its a common term used to describe some sunglasses. By blocking some of the suns brightness and glare, polarized sunglasses can make driving and certain outdoor activities more comfortable. Polarized lenses in sunglasses can help enhance your vision and can also make colors appear more vibrant.

What face shape do cat eye glasses look best on?

Cat eye glasses typically look best on people who have triangle-shaped faces, rectangular faces, or diamond-shaped faces. This is because the contours of the frames complement the angles of these three face shapes.

When are wraparound sunglasses useful?

If your eyes are especially sensitive to dryness when its windy or if the glare from the sun affects your peripheral vision, wraparound sunglasses can help you add a layer of protection on the sides of your face.
