Instead of cooking your entire menu from scratch, select ready-made appetizers, meal kits, and ready-to-heat entrees. Present your pre-made meals like lasagna, rotisserie chicken, pulled pork, or beef ribs on your own serving platters to make the meal special. Which cleaning products should I choose to have an eco-friendly home?

Look for laundry, dish, and household cleaners that are biodegradable. Cleaners and other products that are free of phthalates, sodium lauryl sulfate, ammonia, chlorine, limonene, sodium hydroxide, and perchloroethylene are considered eco-friendly.

I'm on a high protein diet, but I'm tired of shake powder. What are some other high-protein snack options?

Expand your menu with high-protein goodies including chips, cookies, dessert bars, candy cups, trail mix, and granola bars. Try out lemon bars and other high-protein fruit-flavored snacks for a change of pace.
