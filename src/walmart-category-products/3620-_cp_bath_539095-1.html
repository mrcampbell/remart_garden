Bathroom Decor that Stands Out

Whether you're creating a cottage-style bathroom with soothing colors and textures or a bold, contemporary bathroom, there are a variety of items available to help you create the effect you want. Keep the background light and neutral, and then add splashes of color through the decor items and accessories to complete the look. For a cottage-style bathroom, keep the colors soft and subtle. Or go modern with your bathroom decor by choosing black and gray decorations and accessories.


Bathroom furniture options

When you need to make room to store things in the bathroom, there are a variety of options to consider. Consider bathroom cabinets for storage, bathroom shelving with integrated storage space, and bathroom medicine cabinets to store small healthcare and beauty care items. If you don't have a lot of available floor space, consider wall-mounted cabinets, medicine chests, and over-the-toilet cabinets. If you have some open floor space in your bathroom, a storage tower or freestanding cabinet can create a place to put things. These options help keep your bathroom organized by providing convenient storage space that complements your decor.


Shower curtains for privacy and style

In the bathroom, the shower curtain is one of the major decorative elements. This curtain is big and that makes it a focal point in the room. Choose soft, muted colors for a cottage-style bathroom, or pick bold colors for a modern effect. Items to consider from the shower curtain collection include liners, fabric curtains, and sets that provide pre-matched liners and fabric curtains. If you're choosing a liner, consider details like whether you want it to be weighted or magnetic to help it stay closed or antimicrobial to help prevent germs from forming on the surface of the liner.


Bath towels that match your decor

Want to add some color to your bathroom decor? Choosing bath towels, hand towels, washcloths, and sets of towels is a way to do it without making major changes. Match towels and washcloths to the main color in the bathroom for a themed, monochromatic effect. Or choose bath linens in a color that matches one of the other shades in the shower curtain or a picture to draw it out for a fresh style. Choose soft natural fiber towels or microfiber ones to dry off in comfort.


Bath mats and rugs for the bathroom

When you want to keep the floor dry and have a soft place to stand in the bathroom, you may want to add a bath mat or bathroom rug set. Look for safety features, such as a nonslip backing on a rug to put down in a room with slick flooring. For a luxurious effect, consider a memory foam bath mat or set. Your feet sink into the soft rugs, and the rugs also protect flooring by soaking up dripping water.


Bright and absorbent beach towels

For a towel that goes from the bathroom to the beach, browse through the selection of beach towels to find one that fits your style. You'll find playful styles with animal themes, food themes, beach towels with clever sayings on them, and you also have the option to have your beach towels personalized. These towels are larger than bath towels, which makes them ideal for wrapping around your body.


Accessories for the bathroom

The accessories you choose to add to your bathroom enhance the decor and help keep your bathroom organized. These accessories are focused on providing storage and organization. There are also several types of caddies, like toothbrush holders and countertop organizers that help keep your bathroom organized. Choose accessories like soap dispensers and wastepaper baskets that match the style of your bathroom.


Bathroom hooks, rods, and hardware

Stick with your desired decor theme when choosing hooks, rods, and hardware for hanging your shower curtain. These pieces blend into the background but still add to the overall effect in the room. Shower rods are available in straight tension-rod styles or as curved rods. You'll also find double-rods that make it easy to hang the shower liner and fabric shower curtain separately. Choose between solid colors for cottage-style bathrooms, metal hues for modern-style bathrooms, and wood-grained rods for traditional bathrooms to get the specific effect you want.
