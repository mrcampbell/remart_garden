About Breakfast & Cereal - Walmart.com

What can I add to hot or cold cereal to change it up?

Ramp up the flavor by adding toppings like berries, bananas, nuts, apples, and other fruits. Sweeten your cereal with honey, brown sugar, agave nectar, or maple syrup. Spice it up with cinnamon, vanilla, or ginger, or add dairy products like yogurt or butter to warm cereals to make them creamy. Increase your protein intake by adding flavored protein powder into your breakfast cereal.

What nutrients do breakfast cereals contain?

Some cereals are high in fiber, which helps with energy level, regular digestion, and blood sugar levels. Whole-grain cereals may also be high in vitamin B. Some cereals are high in protein, and many cereals are fortified with iron, vitamin D, and calcium.

What other ways can cereal be used in cooking?

Pour dry breakfast cereal into a travel container as a snack. Crush up some kinds of cold cereal to use as breading for chicken or fish. Grind up old-fashioned oats to make oat flour for baking. Mix oatmeal with ground meat as a binder for meatloaf. Add oatmeal to baked treats like oatmeal raisin cookies.
