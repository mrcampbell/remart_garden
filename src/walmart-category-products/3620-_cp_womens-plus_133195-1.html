About Women's Plus Size Clothing | Walmart.com

Women's Plus Size Clothing

Searching for an amazing selection of plus size clothing for women? Discover the many styles, colors and brands waiting for you at Walmart--all designed to fit and flatter the full-figured woman in sizes 0X, 1X, 2X, 3X, 4X and 5X. Let's get shopping!

Start by measuring yourself

To measure yourself properly, you'll want to measure your height, bust size, waist, and hips. Once you know your measurements, it's pretty easy to glance at a size chart for a particular item or brand and know what size you need. Walmart offers everything for the plus size woman including coats and jackets, dresses, tops, sweaters, jeans, activewear, shoes, bras and panties, and so much more in sizes 0X, 1X, 2X, 3X, 4X, and 5X.

How to find the best clothing for your body type

What woman wouldn't want a wardrobe of body-flattering clothes--outfits she can slip into confidently, knowing that her assets will be played up and the areas she loves less will be played down? We have defined body shapes and have made some wardrobe suggestions for you:

  • Apple shape: A plus size woman with an apple body shape has a prominent, curvier upper body. Concentrate on knee-length dresses with a subtle A-line shape, V-neck, or scoop neck tops will create the illusion of a longer torso. Your ultimate outfit: a wrap dress.
  • Pear shape: A plus size woman with a pear body shape usually carries weight on the lower half of the body resulting in full hips, heavy thighs, or a substantial bottom. The clothing that looks best on a pear body type strike a balance between top and bottom. Go for tops with eye-catching necklines like a boat or square neck draw attention upward and short jackets with shape at the waist. Your ultimate outfit: the jumpsuit.
  • Hourglass shape: A plus size woman with an hourglass body shape has a fuller bust and fuller hips with a smaller waistline. Look for clothes that streamlined fashions that follow your natural body line. Choose high-waisted skirts, a pencil skirt with seaming; slim-cut, body-skimming boot-cut, or straight-leg styles with a mid-rise show off your shape. Your ultimate outfit: an empire-waist dress.
  • Inverted triangle shape: A plus size woman with an inverted triangle body shape is widest at the shoulders and bust, while her frame narrows through the hips, thighs, and legs. Direct attention away from the upper half and create balance with wrap tops and V-necks, fit and flare dresses, and wide leg pants. Your ultimate outfit: a tunic with wide-leg pants, finished with a pendant necklace to draw the eye away from shoulders.
  • Rectangle shape: The lines of a plus size woman with a rectangular body shape run straight up and down; weight is distributed evenly on top and bottom. Pick out clothing to create curves and make your waist appear narrower. Wrap tops, deep scoop neck tops, and V-neck tops are perfect for drawing attention downward. Other good picks? Fit and flare dresses, high-waisted skirts, and boot-cut jeans. Your ultimate outfit: a peplum top paired with a pencil skirt.

What are some plus size dressy outfit ideas?

Walmart has an extensive collection of dressy plus-size clothes for women. Celebrate a special evening out by slipping into a stunning cocktail dress or party dress from Terra & Sky, Romantic Gypsy, Love Sadie, Ever Pretty, and more!

Looking for great style at a great value? Shop plus size clothing at Walmart and discover the brands you love: Terra & Sky, Just My Size, Woman Within, Lee, Athletic Works, and more--you'll be tempted to refresh your entire closet!
