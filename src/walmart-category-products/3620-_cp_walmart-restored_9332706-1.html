Frequently asked questions

How does the warranty work for Walmart Restored Premium items?

All Restored Premium items have at least a one-year warranty. For items sold by third party sellers, please use the 'Contact seller' feature on the seller's information page to request a copy of the warranty for this item from the seller.

How do returns work for Walmart Restored items?

Items in Restored condition (not Restored Premium) can be returned or replaced for free within 90 days of purchase. Please contact Walmart customer care or https://www.walmart.com/returns for more details.
