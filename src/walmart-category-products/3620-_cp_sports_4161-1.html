About Sports - Walmart.com

Sports Gear

Get in the game with sports gear. There are many benefits associated with practicing a sport on a regular basis. You might even want start a new sport to get in shape or join a team to meet some new friends. Regardless of the sport that interests you or your child, Walmart has the gear you need at Every Day Low Prices.

Safety first

Safety should always come first when practicing a sport, so it's important to invest in quality gear that'll last. Depending on the sport your child plays, they might need a helmet, a chin guard or another type of protective gear for their head. Be sure to upgrade gear as your child grows and always check to make sure helmets and pads are in good working order to help prevent injuries. If an injury happens, you can help treat it and heal with tape, braces, bandages and pads. You can also shop for compression gear and physical therapy items to help prevent future injuries or sprains.

Bring the sports to you

There are plenty of ways to practice a sport at home. You could install a portable basketball system in your driveway or mount a backboard-and-rim combo on your garage. Soccer nets and roller hockey goal sets can easily be set up in your backyard or in the street. Footballs, baseballs and gloves can be taken on a trip to the park, and volleyballs can be used at the beach.

Discover your new hobby

Are you looking for your next passion? Are you trying to get your children interested in a new activity? You can easily find the gear you need to get started with several sports, such as lacrosse or field hockey. You can also shop for tennis racquets and clothing, ice and roller hockey starter sets, swimming goggles and caps, golf clubs and golf balls and even referee equipment. Whether you work as a referee or volunteer as one for local games, you need to look the part. You'll be able to find everything from shirts and whistles to scoreboards and clocks.

Protect and transport your gear

Quality sports gear can last for years if properly taken care of. While you're on the move, you can store your golf clubs in a golf bag, keep your hockey gear in a duffel bag or transport your soccer cleats and balls in a gym tote. Tennis racquets and lacrosse sticks can be protected by being kept in zip-up bags while not in use.

At Walmart, you can find all the best sports deals so you can Save Money. Live Better.
