PC and Laptop Finder

If you're in the market for a new PC or laptop, Walmart can help. Whether you're going to use it for work, school, entertainment, or all of the above, you'll be able to shop a wide assortment of computers, laptops, and tablets all at everyday low prices. Not sure where to start? Use the Find Your PC to get suggestions for your perfect match.

What kind of PC or laptop should I get?

Here, you'll be able to find a wide assortment of PCs, laptops, ultrabooks, and Chromebooks that can suit your needs. You can shop by brand or by use. Some examples are:

  • Daily use: These laptops can blend in perfectly, whether you're using them for work or for personal use. They're great for checking your email, storing files and photos, and browsing the web.
  • Entertainment: For those who love to stream, here you can find laptops and PCs that can help you create, connect, and binge watch. These are ideal for storing photos and videos, multi-tab browsing, presentations, and are streaming friendly.
  • Work and play: Play well with others with a gaming laptop. Great for both working and playing, gaming PCs and laptops can feature cloud computing, virus protection, and long-lasting battery life.
  • Productivity: You deserve a computer that works as hard as you do. Made for multitaskers, these PCs and laptops can edit photos and videos, collaborate with others, stream content freely, and design and create a multitude of projects.
  • On-the-go: If you're looking for something that you can take with you around the house or office, on the train, or anywhere you go, check out the selection of laptops and tablets that are perfect for on-the-go. Travel light and spend lighter with access to all your apps, browsing from anywhere, and portable video streaming.

Don't forget to check out the selection of PC and laptop accessories, so you can get the most out of your machine. From sleeves and cases to speakers and headphones, you'll be able to find everything you need to work hard and play hard at Walmart.
