About Wedding Shop - Walmart.com

Celebrate your union in style with Walmart's Wedding Shop

From the engagement to the reception, weddings take a lot of planning and require plenty of decisions from the happy couple. Walmart is here to make the entire process easy and fun from start to finish. We have all the jewelry, decor, gifts, invitations and registry items you need at Every Day Low Prices, making it simple to throw the wedding of your dreams without making sacrifices. Weddings are a major life event, but with our helpful products and services, it'll feel like an easy task that's part of one of the happiest times of your life. With a wide variety of options available for you to choose from, you'll find it easy to make your wedding your own and show your guests a good time too.

Wedding registry

Our Wedding Shop includes registry tools that you can use to put together your ultimate gift list for your guests to use. Whether you've been living together for years or you're going to be starting your shared life together on your wedding day, you can find a variety of useful, stylish and fun items among our various product categories. Register for practical things or for something special the choice is yours. There's no wrong way to build a wedding registry, though traditional registries tend to focus on household items like cooking supplies, kitchen gadgets, bathroom accessories and linens. However, couples who live together often don't need these items, so modern registries tend to branch out a bit and focus on other elements the couple wants, like outdoor sports equipment or accessories for entertaining. You can even register for tech equipment like a big TV or gaming console the two of you love so you can play your favorite video games together in wedded bliss.

Wedding planning supplies

Planning your wedding doesn't have to be stressful. Walmart's Wedding Shop has a large selection of useful supplies you can use to plan and carry out the tasks on your to-do list. From stylish invitation stationery and RSVP cards to party supplies and decorations to make your reception and ceremony as fun and magical as possible, you can accomplish a wide range of wedding tasks all in the same place. We can't make big decisions like theme and color choices or guest lists for you, but we can make the process of arriving at those decisions smooth and easy. Our invitations are available in different colors and themes, which can help you make your final choice, and supplies like wedding planner books and organizers make the whole process of putting the event together much easier.

Decorations and party supplies

Our wedding shop has all the wedding-specific party supplies a couple could want to make their ceremony and reception just right for their vision. If you want a traditional wedding with a lot of white and floral decor, we have plenty of different options to consider, from sleek white tablecloths to faux flower petals and crystal accents to add some sparkle to the big day. You can also choose from colorful ceremony and reception elements in different shades, including white, gold, red, pink, purple and blue. Mix and match different accents and elements until you find the exact look you're going for. You can also find decorative elements like DIY tissue paper pompoms or hanging circular fans to add dimension and depth to your decor. You can use some of these items to make a photobooth backdrop to give guests a fun souvenir for the evening or cover up parts of your reception or ceremony venue that aren't as festive as you'd like. We also have pieces like candy bags and mini metal buckets that you can use to make your own party favors and centerpieces for the reception. Of course, you'll probably want some candles to add a romantic atmosphere to the tables at your reception, and we have tea lights, votives and other bulk candle options to suit.

Supplies for the bride

The wedding day is the bride's big moment, and we're ready to help each and every lady say "I do" in style. From bridal lingerie to accessories like shoes and handbags, our Wedding Shop has some bride-focused categories that make it easy to cross bridal-prep activities off your to-do list. You'll even find some fun extra touches like crystal details for shoes and special hair accessories that make your wedding look more personal and unique. And if you're going to be throwing a bridal shower, you'll find plenty of separate elements for that event as well, including special bridal shower invites and decorations. The Walmart Wedding Shop has planning quizzes and other helpful tools that make the bride's job much easier.
