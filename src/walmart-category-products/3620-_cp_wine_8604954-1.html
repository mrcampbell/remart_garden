About Wine - Walmart.com

How do I pair wine with food?

As a basic guide, pair red wine with red meat and pair white wine with chicken, fish, and pasta dishes. A bold red wine complements stronger flavors, such as steak, game, and hard cheeses, while a medium red complements cured meats, hard cheeses, and bread. A light red wine compliments chicken, fish, roasted vegetables, and bread. Light white wines go well with chicken, seafood, and salads, while sweet white wines are perfect with desserts, smoky foods, and salty dishes. White wines that are full-bodied complement pork, chicken, salmon, and potatoes.

Can I make mixed drinks with wine?

Enjoy numerous cocktails made with red, white, rosé, or sparkling wine. Mix sparkling white wine or champagne with fresh orange juice. Make a quick and easy cocktail by adding soda to your favorite wine. Cool down on a hot day with a berry wine slushie made by blending mixed berries, wine, and ice cubes together. Add simple syrup and club soda to the fortified wine sherry and garnish it with fruit to create a sherry cobbler.

How many people does a bottle of wine serve?

A standard bottle of wine contains about 5 servings. Plan on 3 glasses per person for parties.
