About Protein & Fitness - Walmart.com

Fuel your body with protein and fitness supplements

A balanced diet is the foundation of a healthy lifestyle, but sometimes your body needs more than the nutrients it gets from the food you eat. Athletes, vegetarians and vegans, in particular, may need to supplement their meals to make sure they get the right amount of protein, vitamins and minerals their bodies need to function properly. You don't have to go to a specialty store to find nutritional products. Search our selection of protein powder, food and other fitness-related supplements from top brands.

Protein powder

Your body needs protein to build muscle and recover from workouts, but sometimes it's a challenge to eat enough protein-rich foods to meet your daily requirements. Protein powder is a convenient option for anyone who wants or needs to consume more protein. You can find powders based on a variety of sources, including whey and casein as well as hemp and pea protein suitable for vegetarian and vegan diets. Use it as a meal replacement or a post-workout recovery snack.

Protein drinks and food

Protein drinks and food items are another way to boost your protein intake. Replace your afternoon candy bar with a protein-packed cookie or meal replacement bar. Stock up on pre-mixed protein shakes that you can store in your desk drawer or travel bag, so you always have something easy to grab to stave off hunger between meals. When a craving for something crispy strikes, reach for a crunchy, savory snack filled with flavor.

Pre-workout and energy

Fuel up before you head to the gym or go out for a run with pre-workout powders and drinks. You get an energy boost that helps you power through your workout without loading up on sugar. Explore the variety of flavors including citrus and tropical fruit blends along with traditional chocolate and vanilla. You'll also find formulas designed to meet the unique needs of different types of athletes.

Amino acids and sports supplements

Amino acids are the building blocks of protein, but they do even more. They play a role in multiple functions of the body, including hormones, neurotransmitters and enzymes. If you want to make sure you get the essential amino acids and branch chain amino acids your body needs, choose from an assortment of supplements as well as individual amino acids such as arginine, glutamine and creatine in pill or powder forms.

Greens and superfoods

Even if you eat a balanced diet, you may still benefit from extra doses of nutrients found in superfoods. They offer special disease-fighting vitamins, minerals and antioxidants in concentrated amounts. Check out the selection of products including apple cider vinegar, acai berries, spirulina and wheatgrass. Try them in pill, liquid and capsule forms that you can sprinkle in protein shakes, soups and cereal to sneak them in your regular foods, so you get the nutrient boost without sacrificing flavor.

Shop by workout

Save time by restricting your search to products designed for specific workout phases. See all of the powders, bars and snacks that optimize your energy levels and meet your nutrient needs before, during and after your workouts. Start with energy boosters before you hit the weights or the field. Replace the electrolytes and stay hydrated while you sweat. Then give your body the dose of protein it needs to recover and rebuild after you've finished your workout.


Staying in shape and keeping your body healthy is easier when you feed your body the nutrients it needs. Whether you spend all your spare time in the gym or get together friends for team sports on the weekend, supplementing your diet can help you perform better on and off the field.
