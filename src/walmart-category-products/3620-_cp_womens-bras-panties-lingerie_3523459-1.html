14 Bra Types Every Woman Should Know. Considering there are so many different bras to choose from, picking The One can seem like a daunting task. Factor in your size, shape, nudes, and shades and finding the perfect bra for your body type can feel overwhelming. But, once you find the right styles for your figure, you'll be infinitely more comfortable. Walmart has the bras you want from the names you love, plus you'll find our bra size charts are easy to read with bra sizes in order.

    1. Full Coverage Bras. A properly fitted full coverage bra covers all of you. For the right size, be sure the center of your breast is halfway between your shoulder and elbow.
    2. Minimizer Bras. If you want to draw less attention to your breast, a minimizer bra can help reduce projection by up to two inches for a flattering silhouette.
    3. Push-Up Bras. Push-up bras have padding or removable inserts for added lift.
    4. Smoothing Bras. A smoothing bra hides any lumps or bumps for a sleek foundation under T-shirts and tops.
    5. Strapless Bras. An essential bra for off-the-shoulder tops, strapless gowns and more.
    6. Convertible Bras. Also known as multi-way bras offer modesty as well as support with off-the shoulder dresses and bandeau styles.
    7. Performance Bras. For low to high impact activities, we have the perfect sports bras for extra support.
    8. Bonded Bras. Made for high-intensity fitness classes, running, kickboxing and more. Stretchy, wireless support found.
    9. Wirefree Bras. A wirefree bra offers flexible support that contours to your shape--say goodbye to uncomfortable wires!
    10. Bralettes. Lacy, solid, or ribbed bralettes are ideal for sheer and low-cut tops.
    11. Plus-size Bras. We offer bra sizes for the full-figure woman with sizes up to 58M.
    12. Nursing Bras. Nursing bras feature a discreet, clip-down design for easier access and feedings.
    13. Adaptive/Post-Surgical Bras. Ensure comfort and confidence as you move.
    14. Sustainable Bras. Support for you and the planet--check out our new Kindly Yours collection.

Types of Panties. There are so many options to choose from--it's hard to know which type of panty is for what. We're here to help with a list of the different types of panties, their level of coverage and what to wear them with. Discover cotton panties, satin panties, and plus-size panties in reliable nudes and the latest shades at Walmart now!

    1. Thongs. Offer minimal coverage to avoid panty lines. It sits below the waist with a T-shape string at back. Wear thongs with form-fitting skirts, dresses and leggings.
    2. Hipster. Also known as a hip hugger because it sits on the hips and have low-cut leg holes. Covers three-quarter of the cheek. Wear hipsters with low-rise jeans and pants.
    3. Briefs. Designed to be comfortable and provide lots of front and back coverage. Wear briefs with low to high-rise pants.
    4. Bikinis. Similar to a brief with less coverage. The waistband sits below the waist and has high-cut leg holes. Only a hint of cheek shows. Wear bikinis with everything for great comfort.
    5. Boyshorts. Designed after men's briefs with a rectangular shape and low-cut leg. Offer full back coverage. Wear boyshorts with medium to high-rise pants.
    6. High-leg or French-cut. Designed with a high waist and high leg holes--considered a bikini-brief hybrid. Wear high-cut underwear with low-rise jeans or pants.
    7. Control briefs. This women's underwear style offers tummy control and sits high on the waist. There's a hint of cheek at back. Wear control briefs with form-fitting dresses, skirts, leggings and more.
    8. Seamless panties. Also known as No Show panties, offering smooth coverage, usually made in cotton for pillow-soft comfort.
    9. Cheeky panties. Cheeky is a happy medium between the full rear exposure of a thong and the full coverage of a bikini or brief, adding up to practical, versatile comfort with a splash of sexy sophistication.
    10. Sustainable panties. Keeping the planet in mind while staying cool and comfortable. Restock your top draw basic with our new collection of Kindly Yours panties.
