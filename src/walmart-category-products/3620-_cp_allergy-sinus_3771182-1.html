About Allergy and Sinus - Walmart.com

Is it safe to take allergy medicine and sinus medicine at the same time?

Yes, it's generally safe to take both allergy medicine and sinus medicine at the same time, as long as both medicines have been approved by your doctor. Because each type of medicine treats a different nasal symptom, many people find relief from using both at once.

Does allergy medicine always make you drowsy?

Allergy medicine works by reducing the histamines in the body, and one side effect of this process is drowsiness. However, not every allergy medicine makes you drowsy. Look for formulas that are specifically formulated to be non-drowsy if you'd prefer, and always follow the recommended dosage and directions.

When is the best time to take allergy medication?

Allergy medication is most effective when it's given time to work in the body, so taking it the night before tends to be good timing. However, it's always important to double-check with your doctor.
