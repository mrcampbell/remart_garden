About Scrubs - Walmart.com

Nursing and Medical Scrubs

Our scrubs are made to provide maximum comfort and maneuverability for doctors, nurses, technicians,
medical office specialists, homecare workers and more who handle the rigors of everyday patient care.
You will need to own several sets of scrubs to wear every day and you may even need to own a variety
of colors or patterns. How do you keep costs down when you have to invest in your own uniform? Shop for affordable scrubs at Walmart.com. today! Find a wide selection tops, pants, jackets and more for women, men, plus sizes plus, maternity scrubs. 

Top Scrub Brands at Walmart. You’ll find the most comfortable, functional scrubs for medical professionals from the best brands including Scrubstar Scrubs, Koi Scrubs, Jockey Scrubs, Med Couture Scrubs. Grey’s Anatomy Scrubs. Barco One Scrubs, Urbane Scrubs, Landau Scrubs and Cherokee Scrubs.

Types of Scrubs.  As more and more workers wear scrubs, there are lots of high-tech and fashionable scrubs available. Anti-microbial fabrics, flattering cuts, and colorful patterns make medical uniforms more
functional and more fun than ever before…

Fashion Scrubs. From Y-necks and V-neck to wrap styles with side slits, scrubs are made to flatter every body type.

High Tech Scrubs. Tired of trying to find a safe place to store your cellphone? We’ve got you covered with scrub tops with inner pockets. We also carry scrub tops and pants in comfy stretch fabrics that wick away moisture to keep you cool and dry on a long shift.

Scrubs for Men. Here, now—cargo scrubs that feel just like your favorite pants with a  functional drawstring, zip fly and front button closure. You’ll also love all the pockets for stashing your gear.

Unisex Scrub Jackets. You’ll look every inch the professional in a sporty scrub jacket. You’ll also be
nice and warm.

Lab Jackets. The last thing you want to worry about while you do your groundbreaking medical research is clothes. The lab jacket wins for easy style and fabulous function as a top layer with pockets for all of your essentials.

The Amazing Power of Color. The color of the scrubs you wear may have an impact on the mood of patients and coworkers. At Walmart, we offer a huge selection of popular scrub colors, prints and patterns to create a scrub wardrobe that’s comfortable and fashionable.

Blue scrubs symbolize trust, loyalty, confidence, wisdom, truth and faith. Blue is also associated
with stability, cleanliness and technology.

Green scrubs represent the color of nature, relaxation, fertility, growth, harmony, tranquility and safety.

White scrubs are a symbol of purity and faith and associated with cleanliness and safety.

Black scrubs have a lot of mystery and authority associated to them. They are associated with formality
and elegance. Black is known for creating an illusion of a slimmer appearance.

Shopping for medical uniforms on a budget or professional clothing allocation? Price and quality
are important factors in your buying decision. At Walmart, you’ll find top-quality scrubs from your favorite brands at everyday low prices! And, with so many options, you can mix and match scrub styles or stock up on complete sets—all of your favorite colors, styles and fits are easy to find at a price you want at Walmart today!
