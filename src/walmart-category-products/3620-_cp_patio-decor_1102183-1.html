About Patio & Outdoor Decor - Walmart.com

Tips for enhancing your patio and outdoor decor

When you re decorating your home, don t forget about the patio and yard. They provide you with a chance to create a welcoming extra space for unwinding or entertaining. At Walmart, you ll find an incredible selection of patio and outdoor decor essentials at Every Day Low Prices, which means you don t have to rein in your creativity. With the numerous options available in a wide range of colors and finishes, you can easily transform your outdoor space to express your personal style.

Patio furniture

There s nothing better than enjoying your outdoor living space on a warm summer day, and with patio furniture you can be sure you do it in comfort. Whether you're looking forward to an al fresco dinner or you simply want to enjoy a good book on your patio, we have a wide range of outdoor furniture to suit your preferences.

  • Outdoor patio sofas are an excellent choice for those moments when you want to entertain
  • If you want to sunbathe comfortably, look for upholstered lounge chairs that come in many fabrics and styles
  • Large outdoor tables work well for family dinners, whereas small ones are ideal for holding snacks and drinks
  • For extra relaxing options, look at hammocks and outdoor recliners

Lighting

Illuminating your outdoor space is essential for creating that homey atmosphere your family and friends are bound to love. We carry everything, from post mounted lighting so you can illuminate an entrance or path to hanging fixtures, deck lighting and decorative lighting accents.
  • Path lighting is essential for highlighting the walkways in your yard
  • With deck lighting, you can make stairs safer while adding to the ambiance of your outdoor space
  • Hanging fixtures work well for tall porches and entrances
  • Add extra security in the area of exterior doors with outdoor wall lights

pots and planters

Potted plants allow you to amplify your green thumb without the weeding. Plan a structure for your containers, and enhance your outdoor space with our selection of garden vases, plant stands and decorative vases.

  • Pots don t always need to sit on the ground mix it up by suspending them from a wall or by placing them on window ledges
  • Plant stands make it easy to clean around larger pots and enjoy them anywhere in your home during the colder seasons
  • Add an artsy touch to your outdoor space with our decorative bowls and boxes made from plastic or cement

Outdoor heating

Create a snug fireside ambiance after the sun has set so you can enjoy your garden long into the night. Whether you're looking for electric heaters, fire pits or chimeneas, our selection of outdoor heating deliver the exact type of heating you want.
  • electric heaters generate a lot of warmth, but you need an electric outlet to nearby to plug them into
  • With a fire pit, you can get the kind of heat generated by a traditional open fire
  • Chimeneas are a traditional form of outdoor heater made from cast iron, clay or steel that mix function and form beautifully

Fountains

Installing a garden fountain, you can easily create a personal haven for reflection to retreat to at the end of the day. With so many models and styles to choose for, it's easy to add a water accent to your outdoor space.

  • Waterfall fountains are made from slate or river stone and make the water tumble over rocks for a natural effect
  • For a great focal point, go for a large tiered fountain made from granite or stone
  • A birdbath fountain keeps the water fresh while providing a place for birds to drink and bathe
  • If you have the space, a pond fountain makes a classic and vibrant option

Garden statues and decorations

Inject some character into your outdoor space with garden statues that come in all sizes and shapes. Opt for a fun approach by choosing garden gnomes and other lovely ornaments, or go for a more traditional approach with antique or reproduction statues.

  • Add an enchanting touch to your garden with fairies, trolls and gnomes inspired by fairy tales
  • Terracotta and ceramic garden statues don't degrade easily, but are quite fragile
  • For a durable yet charming garden decoration, choose a metal statue
  • Asian-inspired garden statues featuring motifs such as a Buddha or lotuses are ideal for creating a calm Zen garden
