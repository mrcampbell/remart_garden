What are some good ways to liven up salads?

Add cut up apples, strawberries, peaches, and other fruits. Make salads a meal by topping them with sliced egg rolls, spring rolls, or grilled chicken. Whip up your own salad dressings to try new flavor combinations.

What's the best way to cook meat to preserve the nutrients?

Cooking meat at high heat, grilling, or stewing for long periods reduces the B vitamins and other nutrients. To preserve nutrients, use a pressure cooker or sous vide set up. Save meat juices and serve them with meat, because the juices contain some of the B vitamins lost during the cooking process.

How do I know that a melon is ripe?

Ripe cantaloupe turns tan and has a sweet fragrance at its stem. Ripe honeydew melon rind turns creamy yellow, the skin is dull looking, and the non-stem end softens. A watermelon is ripe when it has dull skin, a yellowish underside, and the stem end is dry.
