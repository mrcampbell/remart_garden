About Xbox All Access - Walmart.com

What are the benefits of signing up for gaming consoles and video game access using payment plans?

A chief benefit of using a payment plan to purchase a gaming console and video game access is the low monthly cost. You don't have to save up funds to purchase your console outright. You can try out and play a wide variety of games you don't have to go out and buy or borrow. You get immediate access to new and one-day video game releases too.

Will new video game consoles let me play my older games?

Yes. Newer gaming consoles are backward compatible with many games designed for older consoles, as long as the games are versions from the same brand of gaming system as your newer console. Older games are often graphically enhanced when played on new consoles. Of course, there are some past games that won't play on new consoles. Consult the manufacturer of the console to learn more about the playability of specific games.

Do all video game consoles support 4K video resolution?

No. Some gaming consoles can play videos and other content in 4K, but you can't play video games in 4K on them. If you want to experience 4K video quality when gaming, choose a gaming console that supports 4K resolution.
