About

Children's and Kids' Books

Does your little one love to read? Reading is a wonderful way to relax or to learn new things. Your child can immerse themselves in a story, get to know new characters, or read about their favorite television characters by picking up a book. Walmart has a huge selection of reading material, including classics, new releases, coloring books, learning and workbooks, and picture books for babies and kids at everyday low prices.

Books for every age

Getting your child interested in reading can be as simple as picking up a colorful book that catches their eye. There's a wide selection of fun and colorful books for toddlers, storybooks for young children, and classics including Dr. Seuss or Disney stories for children who are just starting to read by themselves. Encourage your little ones to read on their own by getting books adapted to their reading level. Shop for books featuring your children's favorite characters, such as Peppa Pig or the Avengers, to get them to read. Not sure where to start? Check out the shop by age category to browse all of the age-appropriate book options for your child.

Wizards, fantasy, and sci-fi just for kids

There are so many novels and series to discover in the preteen section, especially in the sci-fi, fantasy, and magic genres. The Chronicles of Narnia or Harry Potter series are great reads for teens who love sci-fi and fantasy. Page-turners from Roald Dahl or Sarah J. Maas are great choices for young teens interested in fiction novels.
