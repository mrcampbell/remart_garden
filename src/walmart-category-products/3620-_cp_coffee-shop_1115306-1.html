About Coffee Shop - Walmart.com

Coffee Shop

Start your morning off right with a cup of coffee. Whether you prefer light roast, dark roast, espresso, lattes, or coffee pods, you'll find everything for the perfect cup at everyday low prices at Walmart.

How do I make the perfect cup of coffee?

If you're new to the wonderful world of brewing your own coffee, it can be overwhelming. With so many to choose from, the first step in selecting the best coffee is finding your preferred taste. Do you like your coffee to have a smooth, sweet taste? Go for a dry, light colored roast. If you prefer a bold, strong coffee, go for a darker roast. If you're looking for something in between, a medium roast is a good compromise. At Walmart, you'll be able to shop from a wide selection of whole bean coffee, ground coffee, and coffee pods for the perfect cup. Keurig and Nespresso pods and machines are great if you're short on time, while whole bean coffee is a good choice if you'd prefer the freshest cup possible. Just make sure to have a good coffee grinder that allows you to choose your grind size.

Next, think about how you'd like to brew your coffee. Some popular methods are with a drip machine, an espresso machine, French presses, pour overs, and percolators. Single-serve coffee makers are also a great option if you're just making a cup for yourself and don't want a whole pot. Coffee and espresso machines are quick and easy with minimal work. With the flip of a switch, you'll have a hot cup of coffee in no time. If you like your coffee with a delicate and smooth finish, try a pour over coffee maker from brands like Bodum or Chemex. If you prefer a heavy, dense cup of coffee, use a press. If you like strong, bold coffee, pick up a percolator. Not sure what to get? Check out our coffee maker buying guide for tips to pick out the perfect drip maker, cold brew maker, or electric kettle.

What else should I get to elevate my coffee?

No matter how you make it, you won't be able to enjoy your cup of coffee if you don't have a coffee cup. Shop the selection of cups and travel mugs to keep your coffee hot (or cold), so you can enjoy it to the fullest. If your preferred method of brewing coffee is a drip machine or a pour over, you'll also need some coffee filters. And if you like milk or cream in your coffee, take it to the next level with a milk frother. Finally, if you want an elegant way to store and display your coffee and accessories, find a coffee cart that matches your sense of style and decor.
