$4 Prescriptions at Walmart Pharmacy

Get the medications you need without breaking the bank. Read on to see some of the most common questions and answers about $4 prescriptions at Walmart Pharmacy.

Is my medication available for $4 prescriptions at Walmart?

Walmart Pharmacy offers some of the most common generic drugs prescribed at commonly prescribed dosages for many different conditions. If you take medications for cholesterol, diabetes, high blood pressure, mental health issues, or thyroid problems, check the drop-down list above to see if your medication is listed. Terms and conditions apply.

How much will my prescription cost at Walmart?

If your medication is on the list, 30-day supplies start at just $4 and 90-day doses start at $10. Prices for some drugs may vary and might be higher in some states, including California and Minnesota. Check out the program details or call your local Walmart Pharmacy for details.

Does Walmart $4 pharmacy include antibiotics?

No, antibiotics, antihistamines, and steroids are not included in the $4 prescription program. See the program details or call your local Walmart Pharmacy to see if your medication is on the $4 prescription list.

Does Walmart Pharmacy do 90-day prescriptions?

Yes, you can get a 90-day supply of common generic medications starting at just $10. Prices and availability may vary by location. Call your local Walmart Pharmacy to see if your prescription is available in a 90-day dose. Terms and conditions apply.

How do I transfer my prescription to Walmart Pharmacy?

Check out our Walmart Pharmacy page to learn how to transfer your prescription to Walmart Pharmacy quickly and easily. Just click "start a transfer" to set up your account online.
