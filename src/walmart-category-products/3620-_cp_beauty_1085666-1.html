A guide to choosing the best beauty products

Taking care of yourself is essential for your well-being, so it s important to choose the right beauty products for your face and body to achieve the results you're looking for. From skincare products that leave your face hydrated and wrinkle-free and hair products for gorgeous locks to oral care for the entire family, you can find all the beauty products you need at Every Day Low Prices at Walmart. Here are some helpful beauty products to stock up on.

Skin care

Whether you're looking for skin care products with anti-aging properties or for a treatment for skin problems such as acne or rosacea, our selection includes something for each skin type. In order for your skin to be radiant and healthy, it's important to use products that are suitable for your skin type. Because each skin type has its own needs, it's a good idea to establish whether you have a normal, dry, oily or combination skin before choosing products geared toward your type.

  • Essential skincare products include skin cleansers and soaps, daily moisturizers with sunscreen, night moisturizers, toners and masks.
  • Sunscreen combats the visible effects of aging that sun damage can cause, so make sure you apply it whenever you go out in the sun. Alternatively, look for daily moisturizers with SPF.
  • Developing a skin care regimen is vital for healthy skin, so start with the basic steps of cleaning, moisturizing, and applying sunscreen every day in the morning. Use a face wash, toner and moisturizer, and apply any treatments or serums after your toner but before your moisturizer. Remember there's no need to use your SPF products at night.

Makeup

Finding the right kind of makeup that suits you perfectly can be daunting, and just like in the case of skincare products, you need to use something that works well with your skin type. If you're searching for foundations and concealers, there's the added step of finding the color that matches your skin tone exactly (or mixing several to get the right shade). Keep your skin tone in mind as you search. To determine it, look at the veins in your arms. If they have a blue tint, you're cool toned; if they have a green tint, you're warm toned and if you have both, neutral shades will work for you.

  • Whether you want to create a natural look for the day or something more dramatic for the evening, there are certain products that are essential for any makeup bag. These include foundation to create a smooth surface, powder to set your makeup, lipstick to accent your mouth, and eyeliner, eyeshadow and mascara to define your eyes.
  • Use the right cosmetic tools to apply your makeup to get the best results. A couple of essentials include a blush brush to apply powder and blush, an eyelash curler to define your lashes before applying mascara, a good make-up mirror so you can see all the details and a makeup remover or toner to eliminate all residue from your skin without drying it out.

Hair products

When you think about hair products, shampoo and conditioner the first things to come to mind, but there are even more products available to enhance the look and texture of your locks.

  • Leave-in conditioner helps you untangle and comb your hair easily, while hair oil can keep the ends frizz-free and polished.
  • If you blow-dry or straighten your hair regularly, don't forget to use a heat-protection spray to keep your hair feeling soft and moisturized.
  • Hairspray and wax are excellent for creating hairstyles that last through the day or night.

Fragrances

Whether you're looking for a fragrance for women or men, determine the notes that you prefer in the overall scent. You can also choose different formulations; eau de parfums are the strongest and have the longest staying power, eau de toilettes are lighter than eau de parfums and body splashes and sprays are light without as much staying power.

  • All perfumes have three different layers called base, top and middle notes. These all work together to create a particular smell. Common choices include floral, fruity, exotic, or musk scents.
  • Always pay attention to the description of a perfume to determine whether it s a good choice for your lifestyle and personality.

Oral care

Maintain your family's oral health with products that help them keep their teeth clean and their breath fresh every day. From toothpaste and mouthwash to toothbrushes and water picks, our selection of oral care products has something suitable for all ages.

  • Toothpaste comes in many forms, ranging from gel and paste formulations to spearmint and cinnamon flavors, so it s important to choose one that suits your teeth. For example, if you have sensitive teeth, you may want to use a toothpaste designed specifically to reduce the pain associated with this condition.
  • Mouthwash is essential for protecting your mouth from gum disease and fighting bacteria, with fresh breath being an added benefit.
  • Water picks can help you keep you remove any residue from braces or other kind of dental work that tends to trap food.
