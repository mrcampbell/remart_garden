About Cheese and Snacks - Walmart.com

How long does cheese last in the fridge?

If your cheese isn't opened, you can look at the expiration date or the cheese itself before you eat it. If you have opened it, soft cheeses can be stored in the fridge for about a week, while hard cheeses might last up to a month. Of course, all types of cheese are different, so it's best to make sure what you eat does not have any mold, no matter how long it's been in your fridge.

What is the best type of cheese for my favorite dishes?

American, fontina, and cheddar are great for grilled cheese sandwiches. You can't go wrong with mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses for pizza and other Italian dishes. For your burgers and other sandwiches, top them with cheddar, provolone, Swiss, or Monterey jack. Cheddar and Parmesan are most common for mac and cheese, but if you want to switch things up, try smoked Gouda, Brie, or Gruyere.

Should I buy block cheese or shredded cheese?

Whether you buy shredded or block cheese depends on your preferences. Of course, it's more convenient to buy already shredded cheese when you need it rather than shredding up a block for dishes like tacos and salads, but some argue that block cheese tastes better. A block of cheese will also go further when you shred it yourself compared to a bag of shredded cheese of the same size. The block also typically has a longer shelf life.
