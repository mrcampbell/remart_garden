How should I wash plastic cutlery?

If you plan to reuse plastic cutlery, hand wash it in soapy, warm water and rinse well. Allow to air-dry. Generally, plastic cutlery isn't designed to be reused again and again because when it degrades it can collect bacteria and/or leach toxic chemicals into your food. To protect the earth and stay safe, recycle plastic cutlery after use.

Will peel-n-stick wallpaper adhere to textured walls?

Unfortunately, peel-n-stick wallpaper doesn't adhere well to walls that are very textured and even though it will adhere to lightly textured walls, the texture often shows through the paper. Sanding textured walls can help prepare them for peel-n-stick wallpaper.

How do I find dog clothing in the right size?

Most dog clothing comes with a size chart. To find the right size for your dog, measure the distance from the bottom of the neck to the base of the tail and compare it to the sizes on the chart. For comparison, XXS often fits toy breeds like Yorkies and fox terriers, M works well for beagles and heelers, and L makes the best choice for retrievers and German Shepherds.
