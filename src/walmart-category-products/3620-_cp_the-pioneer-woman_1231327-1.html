About Shop Holiday Deals on The Pioneer Woman - Walmart.com

Pioneer Woman Cookware, Dinnerware and Bedding

Walmart.com is proud to offer the Pioneer Woman line of bedding, kitchen, cooking and dinnerware products. This line of products will equip an entire kitchen, from beautiful bowls to cast iron classics; set your next dinner party for success with inviting dinnerware designs; and turn your bedroom into a sleep sanctuary with floral prints, boho-chic designs and textured fabrics that pamper.

The Pioneer Woman (aka Ree Drummond) is an award-winning food blogger and TV personality, and she shares her expertise on whisks, pots, pans, wooden spoons, plates, knives, measuring cups, pitchers and more with Walmart. And you can add a dash of that signature Pioneer Woman style to your bedroom too, thanks to The Pioneer Woman Bedding Collection. Ree has hand-picked quilts, pillows, duvets and blankets that lend themselves to easy mixing and matching, with bold prints and colors that coordinate. "Life is busy these days, and I think having a cozy, inviting bedroom is the secret to sanity," says Ree. She has been involved with each and every piece to make sure they're all pretty, practical, functional and in line with her personal style. In her words: "I want every single piece to be something I myself would drool over."

Get creative with versatile kitchen pieces designed with real life in mind. Set a perfect table for any occasion with Ree's fun and gorgeous kitchen and dining pieces that go right from oven to table. Get inspired by Ree's product catalog and learn to cook like a Pioneer Woman with her cookbook. Then, deck out your bedroom with Pioneer Woman-approved bedding!

Functional cast iron pots, pans and griddles are must-haves that are made to last. Beautiful bowls add a fun touch to the kitchen — the more the merrier. Throw pillows add a pop of color to your bed or couch; comfy quilts sport down-home designs; luxurious ruched blanets get you cozy quick; and other bedroom essentials round out your Pioneer lifestyle. And best of all, the bedding, kitchen and dining pieces are mix-and-match, so you'll get a chic look every time.

Find everything you need for cooking, dining, lounging, sleeping and entertaining with the Pioneer Woman and Walmart. Save money. Live better.
