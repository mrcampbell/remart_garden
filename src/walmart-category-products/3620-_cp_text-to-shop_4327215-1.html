What can I do with Walmart Text to Shop?

With Walmart Text to Shop, you can take care of a whole shopping trip!

All the basics:
• Search for groceries, household items, and anything else sold through Walmart's website or app
• Manage what's in your Walmart cart by adding or removing things
• Schedule a pickup or delivery time or even confirm the timing for a shipping item
• Checkout with only a few steps

Some other fun things to try:
• If you're on an Apple device, you can text 'reorder' to pull up what you've purchased most regularly. Tap through to quickly add to cart
• Manage a generic list of items by texting 'add to list' with the things you search for - 'string cheese add to list'. You can come back later and text 'shop my list' and the Walmart Text to Shop bot will give you options that are in stock so you can easily add to cart

Any changes you make to your Walmart cart via text will also be reflected on the website and in the app - it's all one cart!

Who am I texting through Walmart Text to Shop?

The Walmart Text to Shop bot responds to your texts. When you text the bot, be specific so it can better understand you!

How do I enroll for Text to Shop?

Click here to sign up for Apple and click here to sign up for Android. Follow the instructions provided step-by-step from there!

Text to Shop requires that you log into your Walmart.com account, tell us what phone number or Apple account you want to link it to and accept our Terms of Service & Privacy Policy.

I lost track of my messages with Text to Shop, what number do I text?

From your Apple device, click here, otherwise, from any other device, text (205) 898-9526. Send Walmart a message like 'Start' to pick up where you left off.

I have a new Walmart account, will Text to Shop still work?

Yes it will but make sure to re-enroll so we know what cart and favorites to pull for you! Click here to sign up for Apple and click here to sign up for Android. Follow the instructions provided step-by-step from there!

I have a new phone, how can I re-enroll in Text to Shop?

From your Apple device, click here, otherwise, from any other device, text (205) 898-9526. Send Walmart a message like 'Start' to pick up where you left off.

You'll be sent a link and asked to sign in to your Walmart.com account and accept Text to Shop's Terms of Service & Privacy Policy.

Does Text to Shop work with Walmart+?

You bet! All Walmart+ benefits like same-day delivery and free shipping with no minimum apply when you use Text to Shop.

How is Text to Shop different from the Walmart App?

Text to Shop is another easy way to shop Walmart. It pulls from your purchase history, recent orders, and favorites to curate suggestions to help you find what you need faster. Because everything goes to the same Walmart cart, customers can use Text to Shop to keep track of what they need, especially while on the go. You can also place your order directly over text!
