About CoComelon - Walmart.com

Can I purchase items and have Walmart send them as gifts?

Many Walmart items are available to be delivered as gifts, though pick-up items are not. When checking out with your order, check the box that says, " My order is a gift." Enter and confirm the recipient's address, and email address, and include a personal message. The message will be delivered via email, while the package will be delivered directly to the recipients address.

How do I find discounted or restored products available at Walmart?

You can find discounted items on any product page by using the filter, special offers, on the left-hand side. In addition, Walmart sells restored electronics and games that you can find simply by searching the title plus the word, "restored".

How do I know if a toy is suitable for my child?

Product pages featuring toys can be filtered according to the age of your child using the filter options on the left-hand side of the page. Using the recommended age range for each toy is a good place to start, but be sure to read product details carefully to check for yourself whether a toy is a good match for your child.
