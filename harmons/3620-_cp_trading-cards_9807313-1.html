About Trading Cards - Walmart.com

How should I store or display my trading cards?

The storage strategy that works best for you will likely depend on what you’re buying and using trading cards for. If you’re collecting them, you might consider getting sleeves, binders, or card storage boxes to protect the cards. If you're buying them to display the cards, a shadow box, hanging display case, or framed display can show off the collection. For trading cards that you use to play a game or trade with friends, any storage option that helps you keep them organized, like a storage box or binder, can help you keep track of what you’ve got on hand.

What’s the difference between gaming cards and sports trading cards?

Gaming cards are typically used to buy and trade with other players to build decks for playing against each other. They may be collected, but are more commonly used as part of the game. Sports cards are often used as memorabilia and collectibles, both autographed and not autographed.

How long will it take to get my trading cards once I complete my order?

Shipping time frames vary. Many of our trading cards are eligible for 2-day shipping, while others are eligible for 3+-day shipping. Your shipping options will be listed in the trading card you choose, with an opportunity to confirm the arrival date and costs before checking out.
