What is the difference between OLED and QLED?

OLED stands for "organic light-emitting diode," and the pixels emit their own light. QLED stands for "quantum dot LED," and the pixels rely on the LED backlight with more similarities to LCD screens. OLED screens tend to have improved contrast and a wider range of viewing angles compared to QLED while QLED screens tend to be brighter than OLED.

All QLED TVs are created by Samsung.

What is HDR?

HDR stands for high dynamic range. This can enhance the TV brightness, contrast, and color to help the picture appear more real life like.

What is 5G and what are the benefits?

5G is the fifth generation mobile network. It is faster and able to handle more connected devices than the existing 4G LTE network. Speeds up to 10x faster than 4G networks.

Benefits

1. Higher performance vs. existing 4G LTE standards

2. Faster download speeds for all your wireless needs

3. More connected devices with faster signals

4. Quick responsiveness and less lag when browsing

5. Enhanced mobile broadband
