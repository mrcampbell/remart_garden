5 Types of Jean Fits:

  1. Slim jeans Not too tight, not to lose. Straight through the hip, slim through the thigh with a narrower leg opening.
  2. Skinny jeans The latest addition to the jeans family, this style is a tighter fit with a tapered leg opening.
  3. Regular jeans Straight leg jeans, generally with a mid-rise. The trouser legfalls straightfrom the knee to hem, with a large leg opening.
  4. Relaxed fit jeans A loose fit from the waist to the leg opening. A relaxed cut denim is not going to hug any part of your body.
  5. Boot Cutjeans A baggy fit. The roomiest fit of jeans provides plenty of space in the thighs, legs and back for men who need it.

In the Swim of Things

It's time to start thinking about swimsuits. At Walmart, its easy to find mens swimsuits that keep you comfortable, allow for freedom of movement and help you look and feel awesome. Lets dive right in.

Swim Trunksare the most common men's swimwear in North America. Mens swim trunks look like shorts but are made from light, fast-drying materials (usually nylon or polyester) and feature a tighter-fitting lining inside the shorts.

Short Trunks with an inseam between 5 and 6 inches, display enough thigh and are great forbeach activities like swimming, jogging, running, volleyball or lying around on a lounge chair or towel.

Mid-thigh Trunks with a 78-inch inseam is not too skippy, not too covered up,so in other words, foolproof. Every guy should have at least one.

Board Shorts. Board shorts are known as the main choice for diehard surfers... these swim trunks are basically a longer version of swim trunks that hit at or past the knee. Originally designed for all sports that include a board, they also offer great comfort and ease for men who burn more easily in the sunthe extra length helps to protect those thighs from scorching!

Style tip: Try wearing your board shorts with a rash guard for men to keep you enjoying the water without compromising on protection.
