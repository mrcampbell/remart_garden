Once I open a package of beef jerky, how long is it safe to eat?

Most beef jerky needs to be consumed three to four days after opening. If you refrigerate it after opening, jerky can last up to a few months. Always check the package of beef jerky for the manufacturer's recommendations.

What's the conversion between fresh herbs and dry herbs?

Use three times as many fresh herbs as you would dry herbs. When following a recipe, one tablespoon of fresh herbs equals one teaspoon of dried herbs.

Which candy is least likely to trigger allergies in kids?

Many of the gummy and sour candies, like candy worms and fish, are allergen-free. Licorice and jelly beans are generally free of major allergens too. Avoid candies made with nuts, eggs, milk, wheat, or soybeans.
