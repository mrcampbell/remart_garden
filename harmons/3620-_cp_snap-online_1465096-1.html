FAQ on Accounts & Eligibility

Does Walmart take food stamps?

SNAP (Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program) is the program formerly referred to as food stamps. Walmart accepts SNAP benefits via EBT cards at all participating Walmart online pickup & delivery locations. Walmart stores also accept SNAP benefits.

Does Walmart accept both EBT SNAP & EBT Cash for online transactions?

Yes, Walmart accepts both EBT SNAP & EBT Cash for online transactions in all states for Shipping, Pickup, or Delivery where available.

Who qualifies for SNAP?

SNAP provides nutrition benefits to supplement the food budget of needy families. Potential participants must apply in the state they live & meet certain requirements to qualify. Learn more about SNAP eligibility on the USDA SNAP recipient website:

https://www.fns.usda.gov/snap/supplemental-nutrition-assistance-program

Do I need to have an account to use EBT for online orders?

Yes, you need a Walmart account to place an online order for pickup, delivery or shipping. Learn more about ordering online here:

https://www.walmart.com/cp/1987240

Can I use EBT on the Walmart shopping app?

Yes, you can pay with your EBT Card when checking out on the Walmart app. Add your card to the account wallet before checking out or add your card during checkout. You will be able to enter your PIN to securely complete payment.

How do I sign up to use my SNAP EBT benefits on Walmart?

Using your SNAP benefits on your EBT card is easy at Walmart. Create an account & add your EBT card info to your account wallet or enter it at checkout. You can search or explore the site for eligible items marked as "EBT eligible.” Once you're ready to check out, add or select your EBT card, check your balance or adjust your payment amount as needed then check out securely with your SNAP EBT PIN.

Do I need a Walmart+ membership to use my SNAP EBT card on Walmart?

You do not need a Walmart+ membership to use your SNAP benefits. Membership has savings & benefits, such as unlimited scheduled home delivery with no delivery fees, & free unscheduled shipping.

Can I use WIC online?

We currently do not support WIC as a payment method to complete online orders. You may use WIC benefits in participating store locations.

FAQ on Shopping & Checkout

Can I use my SNAP/EBT benefits to place an online order?

SNAP benefits can be used for online orders of eligible items placed for pickup, delivery & shipping. Learn more about ways to get your order here:

https://www.walmart.com/cp/1987240

What items are eligible for purchase with SNAP EBT funds?

You can buy items that meet USDA SNAP eligibility guidelines. For the current list of USDA SNAP-eligible food items, visit:

https://www.fns.usda.gov/snap/eligible-food-items

How can I tell which items are EBT SNAP-eligible on Walmart?

Look for the "EBT-eligible" badge when you search on Walmart or visit the item page. You will also be able to check your cart for SNAP eligibility once your SNAP EBT card has been added to your account wallet.

What are my SNAP funds not eligible to cover?

Not all items qualify for SNAP. For the current list of USDA SNAP-eligible food items, visit:

https://www.fns.usda.gov/snap/eligible-food-items

Only items shipped & sold by Walmart.com are eligible to be paid with SNAP. Marketplace items are not eligible for SNAP benefits.

How do I check my SNAP EBT/EBT Cash balance?

You can check your balance in your account wallet or at checkout. To check your balance in your wallet, go to your account & view the card on file. To check your balance at checkout, edit the payment amounts & cards at Review Payment. You must securely enter your PIN before viewing balance.

Can I buy non-SNAP-eligible items with an alternative form of payment?

Yes, you can buy non-SNAP-eligible items in the same order as SNAP-eligible items. At checkout, you will be able to pay with your EBT card & another form of payment. Please note, only one EBT card can be added & used per order.

Is there a minimum order amount to use my SNAP funds?

No, there is no minimum order amount to use your SNAP benefits for pickup, delivery or shipping.

Why are some grocery food items not eligible to be paid with SNAP?

Only items shipped & sold by Walmart.com are eligible to be paid with SNAP & EBT Cash benefits. SNAP benefits cannot be used to buy items sold by Marketplace sellers.

How do I check out with my SNAP EBT/EBT Cash card?

If your SNAP EBT card is already added to your account wallet & you have eligible items in your basket, you will see the card listed as a payment method on the Review Order screen. Otherwise, select "Edit payment" at Review Order & "Add a new payment method" to add your EBT card. There is a maximum of one EBT card allowed per account.

Can I make an edit after placing my order with SNAP EBT/EBT Cash?

Yes, you can make changes to a pickup or delivery order when it is still in the processing stage. Changes include adding or removing items or item quantities, editing substitution preferences or rescheduling your timeslot or store location.

Can I pay for the delivery using SNAP funds?

No, delivery fees & other associated charges may not be paid for with SNAP benefits. Only eligible food may be purchased with SNAP benefits. You may pay for fees using your EBT cash benefits or another payment type.

What if I don't want to use all my SNAP benefits or don't have enough funds to cover my order balance?

If you don't want to use all your available SNAP funds, you can edit the amount you want to apply to the order by editing your payment method. If you don't have enough available SNAP funds to cover your eligible items, you may add another payment type to complete the order. You have the option to split your payment between an EBT card & another form of payment.

What is Weight Debit?

A 10% weight debit surcharge is added to cover some items, such as select fruit & meat items, that are priced by weight. The final price is determined after associates have picked & weighed the items. If the weight debit is more than the final price, you will be refunded the difference.

How do I get free shipping or delivery?

You do not need a Walmart+ membership to use your SNAP benefits. Membership has savings & benefits, such as unlimited scheduled home delivery with no delivery fees, & free unscheduled shipping.

FAQ on Substitutions & Refunds

What if my items go out of stock?

Substitutions are available on pickup & delivery orders. If an item has been paid using SNAP EBT & we must substitute it with an item of higher price, you’ll pay the price of the original item.

You can update your substitution preferences before or after checkout. Learn more about substitution preferences here:

https://www.walmart.com/cp/customer-choice/3893115

Can I return food items?

For SNAP orders with a receipt, an item can be refunded to SNAP EBT account or exchanged for another unit of the same item. Return of funds by cash, credit or gift card is not allowed. See additional details on our return policy here:

https://corporate.walmart.com/policies#return-policy
