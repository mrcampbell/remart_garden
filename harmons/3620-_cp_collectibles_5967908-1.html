About Collectibles - Walmart.com

Where can I buy collectible action hero figures?

You may think that you have to go to a specialty store or engage in an online bidding war to purchase collectible action figures, but you might be surprised to find that many major retailers sell collector items both in-store and online. Depending on the action figure, you can buy new collector items to add to your collection.

How should I preserve a collectible doll?

Collectible dolls need to be well preserved to prevent discoloration of the doll or their attire. There are several ways to preserve collectible dolls. You can choose from glass cases, plastic containers, or even containers that are designed specifically for the preservation of dolls. You can keep the doll in original packaging, but make sure that box is well sealed.

What is a keepsake ornament?

Keepsake ornaments are not your typical holiday ornament. Keepsake ornaments typically have some sort of special value. They may be a replica of a special character, personalized, or limited edition. Keepsake ornaments can be a collectors item or just a customized ornament that has a personal meaning or significance.
