About Artificial Plants and Flowers - Walmart.com

Artificial plants & flowers

If you love the way plants and flowers pull together a room but you don't have time to maintain living plants, consider artificial versions. Made from silk, plastic, or fabric, synthetic greenery provides the color and texture you want without the hassle of watering and finding the best sources of light. They're an ideal option for decorating both indoor and outdoor spaces at your home or office. From houseplants and single stems of flowers to freestanding trees and potted bunches of flowers, find the finishing touches you need for any occasion.

Flowers

There's a reason flowers are a staple in home decorating; they quickly freshen up and brighten a room. If you're looking for a way to add color and texture to a room, explore the selection of artificial flowers. Bring a hint of romance to a traditional bedroom or shabby chic living room with peonies and roses in delicate shades of pink. Fill the kitchen or family room with bright, positive energy from daisies and sunflowers. Give any room a touch of exotic drama with boldly colored lilies and orchids.

Look for bunches and bundles of flowers to fill out an arrangement, or choose single flowers to combine in your own display. Shop by color if you're looking for a particular shade to contrast or coordinate with other accessories in the room. Sort the options by material or container if you want something specific like silk irises or bundles of tulips to put in your own pots and vases.

Greenery panels

Greenery panels give you instant privacy inside and outside your home or office. Use them to block unwanted views of the neighbors' yards or disguise an unattractive fence. Bring some greenery indoors without all the mess. Place panels on walls to give them interesting texture, or cut panels to fit inside assorted picture frames to make works of art your guests will notice. At your next party, set up panels to use as backdrops for photos or to create intimate nooks for your guests to gather.

Plants and trees

If you're looking for lush tropical foliage, chunky succulents, or cascading leafy stems, check out our inventory of artificial plants and trees. Find the perfect ferns to hang along your front porch. Pick up a collection of succulents to line the window sill or perk up the bathroom counter. Choose a towering bamboo or fiddle leaf fig to bring a touch of the tropics into your living room or bedroom. For ultimate ease, look for trees and plants that come in decorative pots or transfer them into your own pots and baskets to create a streamlined look in the room.

Topiaries

From spheres and cones to playful shapes, the selection of topiaries includes shrubs and trees to suit any decorating style. Give the front door traditional curb appeal with a matching set of globe-shaped boxwood topiaries. Bring some dimension and elegance to a room with English ivy. Break up sharp corners with the gentle curves of spiraling cypress or cedar. Mix and match topiaries and group them on a tabletop to bring a touch of greenery into any room.

Wreaths

Don't wait until the winter holiday season to show off your favorite wreaths. Use them to add splashes of color inside your home. Update the front door to reflect the changing seasons or upcoming holidays. Browse the collection and discover an assortment of floral and green wreaths in every imaginable color combination, from soft pink peonies to bold golden forsythia. If you're feeling crafty, pick up a ring of straw or grapevine and create your own wreath.

Fruits

When you're decorating, the tiniest detail can have a dramatic effect in the room. Add pops of color to the kitchen and dining room with pieces of fruit. Stack lemons and limes in a glass jar. Pack a wooden bowl with an assortment of bananas, apples, and oranges. Give garlands of grapevines a touch of authenticity by attaching bunches of grapes to the vine. Create your own fruit-themed topiaries to adorn the kitchen.

Create an inviting space full of character using artificial plants and flowers. You get the look you want without the upkeep. To save even more time, check out our convenient shipping options that bring your selections right to your front door.
