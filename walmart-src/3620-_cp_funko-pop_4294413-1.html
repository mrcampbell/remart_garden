What are some benefits to buying collectible vinyl figures?

While you might buy a figure as a gift or toy for a child to play with, you might also consider the value of collecting vinyl figures for yourself. These include adding limited editions to your collection, which may increase in value in the future. Vinyl figures tend to be an inexpensive way to get into collecting as a hobby too. You might also enjoy collecting as many figures as you can that follow one of your favorite themes, such as sports, musicians, superheroes, or movie characters.

What are some nice ways to display vinyl figures?

Vinyl figures often look great displayed on rows of shelves that hang on your wall. Thin, ledge shelves are helpful for keeping your figures from falling off. Shelves made in the shape of a zigzag add some artistic expression to your display. You can also buy or make a glass-enclosed display case to keep your collection dust-free. Or, display your figures on a bookcase or action figure stands.

Should I take my vinyl figure out of the box?

If your sole plan is to allow your figure to appreciate in value, you probably want to keep it in the box and sealed. If you would like to enjoy the aesthetic of seeing your figures on display, playing with them, or sharing them, by all means, unbox them.
