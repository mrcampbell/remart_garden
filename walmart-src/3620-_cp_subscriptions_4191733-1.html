Subscription FAQ

How can I start using subscriptions?

Subscriptions are available on thousands of everyday favorites such as pet supplies, pantry staples, home cleaning and more. If the item is eligible, you will be able to select “Subscribe to this item” & choose how often you want your item. To participate, you must have a valid Walmart account, shipping address & payment method on file. Sign into your account or create a new account to get started.

How much do subscriptions cost?

There is no charge to subscribe to items. Shipping is free on orders over $35 ($6.99 shipping fee applies to orders below $35 order minimum).

Walmart+ members can start using subscriptions at no added cost to membership & get free shipping on all orders.

How do I skip my subscriptions?

You can easily manage your subscriptions in the app or online. Log in to your account & select Item Subscriptions. Select “Skip” on the subscription you wish to skip.

How do I cancel my subscriptions?

You can easily manage your subscriptions in the app or online. Log in to your account & select Item Subscriptions. Select “Edit” on the subscription you wish to cancel. Select “Cancel subscription”.

How do I change the frequency on my subscriptions?

You can easily manage your subscriptions in the app or online. Log in to your account and select Item Subscriptions. Select “Edit” on the subscription you wish to change. Select the arrow next to frequency. Update the frequency to your requested change.

How do I change the day of the week my subscriptions deliver?

You can easily manage your subscriptions in the app or online. Log in to your account & select Item Subscriptions. Select “Change delivery day” at the top of the screen. Choose your new delivery day.

Do subscriptions include a discount?

Walmart provides everyday low pricing. Subscriptions will not include an additional discount on top of our everyday low price.

Why did the price of my subscription item change?

Prices may decrease or increase on your items depending on the price on Walmart.com the day your order is processed.

Can I subscribe to all items on Walmart.com?

No, not all items are available for subscription service. Please see the individual item page for details.

How do I return a subscription order?

Subscription orders follow normal return processes.
