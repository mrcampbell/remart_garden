What programs are available on the Spark Good Nonprofit portal?
Round up
Sign up for Round Up so customers can support you at checkout on Walmart.com or in the app.
Registry
Create a nonprofit registry with a list of products customers can purchase and ship to your location of choice.
Request Space
Request space in front of your local Walmart to raise funds and drive awareness.
Grants
Apply for grants through your local Walmart or Sam's Club or receive grants when associates submit their volunteer hours.
As a nonprofit, how do I signup to participate in Spark Good?
1. Get Frontdoor verified
Get verified with our third party verification provider, Front Door and setup electronic payment preferences.
2. Create Walmart account
Create a Walmart.com account to log into your Spark Good portal using your FrontDoor verified email address.
3. Participate
Engage with customers and associates through Spark Good programs.
4. Share
Share your impact with your donors on social media, newsletters, or via email. #walmartsparkgood
