Testing & Treatment for Strep Throat, Flu & COVID-19

Feeling sick? Our pharmacists now provide same day diagnostic testing and prescription treatment so you can feel better fast.

Schedule now
Easy online scheduling. Walk-in appointments are also welcome.
Available in: Alaska, Arkansas, Colorado, Florida, Idaho, Iowa, Kansas, Kentucky, Michigan, Minnesota, Nevada, South Dakota, Tennessee & Washington
Currently not accepting insurance. Services may be covered by your HSA, FSA or HRA. Eligibility may vary. Contact your pharmacist for details.
*Available at select locations. Ask your pharmacist if you are eligible.

How it works

Step 1: Assessment

Review of your symptoms and verification of eligibility.

Step 2: Test

Your pharmacist may conduct up to three diagnostic tests.

Step 3: Treatment

For eligible cases, your prescription will be filled same day.

Pricing

Assessment: $70

A Pharmacist will conduct the required assessment to review symptoms and confirm eligibility before any tests are performed.

Strep throat test: $59

A strep throat test involves a swab of the back of the throat.

Flu test: $88

A flu test involves collecting a respiratory sample, through a swab taken from the nose or throat.

COVID-19 test: $88

A COVID-19 test involves collecting a respiratory sample through a swab taken from the nose.

Treatment: Starting at $4

Prescription prices will vary and may be covered by insurance.

Symptoms, transmission, testing & treatment

Strep throat

Symptoms include pain when swallowing & a sore throat.

Flu

Symptoms include fever, chills, muscle ache & fatigue.

COVID-19

Symptoms include fever, cough & loss of taste or smell.

FAQ

Not feeling well?

Get tested at a participating pharmacy right away. Taking the appropriate medication for flu, strep, or COVID-19 early in the course of illness may reduce the severity and duration of symptoms and lesson the risk of serious complications. Symptoms of these illnesses are often similar, so it’s important to be tested in order to receive the right treatment.

How much does this service cost?

Flu Assessment and Test: $158

COVID-19 Assessment and Test: $158

Strep Assessment and Test: $129

Additional tests can be administered if needed, and range in cost from $59–$88 each.

Prescription treatment costs are additional and may be covered by your insurance.

Do you take insurance?

We do not currently accept insurance for Test & Treat services. Testing services at Walmart may be covered by your HSA, FSA or HRA. If you receive a prescription treatment, it may be covered by your insurance.

How quickly can I be seen?

You may schedule an appointment in as soon as two hours, or walk in any time the pharmacy is open.

Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are not required. Feel free to walk in to any of the Walmart Pharmacy locations in the states listed below and ask for the Test & Treat service.

How can I schedule an appointment?

To schedule an appointment, click the "Schedule now" button at the top of this page or call your local pharmacy to schedule an appointment over the phone.

Will I be able to get my test results the same day?

All tests provided are rapid, molecular point-of-care tests, with results available as quickly as 15 minutes.

Will you have to contact my doctor for a prescription?

For all patients who test positive, the pharmacist will be able to prescribe treatment, as long as there are no contraindications. Our pharmacists will contact patients who are prescribed treatment within 72 hours to follow up and check on how they are feeling. For patients who test negative, the pharmacist will speak with them about symptom management and next steps. If a prescription is given, your primary care provider will be notified.

Can children be tested?

Yes, children as young as 3 years old can be tested for flu and strep. Children as young as 12 years old can be tested for COVID-19. Age restrictions vary by state—contact your pharmacist for more details.

Are there people who can’t receive this service?

People who are pregnant or breastfeeding are unable to receive Test & Treat services for flu and strep at their local Walmart pharmacy. Other restrictions may also apply. If you think you are having a medical emergency, contact your doctor or call 911 immediately.

Where is this service available?

Test & Treat is available at all Walmart Pharmacies in the following states: Arkansas, Colorado, Florida, Idaho, Iowa, Kansas, Kentucky, Michigan, Minnesota, South Dakota, Tennessee & Washington
